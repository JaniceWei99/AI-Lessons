10.2 演示准备10.2 Presentation Prep

一个好的演示就像讲一个好故事——有开头、中间和结尾。你需要让观众明白你做了什么、为什么做、以及怎么做的。按照下面的 5 步结构来准备你的演示： A great demo is like telling a great story — it has a beginning, middle, and end. You need to help the audience understand what you built, why you built it, and how you built it. Follow this 5-step structure to prepare your presentation:

1

提出问题The Problem

你的 App 解决了什么问题？为什么这个问题很重要？What problem does your App solve? Why does this problem matter?

2

解决方案The Solution

你的 App 是怎么解决这个问题的？用一句话概括。How does your App solve this problem? Summarize in one sentence.

3

现场演示Live Demo

在手机上展示你的 App，展示核心功能。Show your App on your phone, demonstrate the core features.

4

技术介绍Tech Stack

简单介绍你用了哪些技术（App Inventor + 什么 AI API）。Briefly explain what technologies you used (App Inventor + which AI API).

5

未来计划Future Plans

你接下来还想给 App 加什么功能？What features do you want to add to your App next?

时间控制：3-5 分钟

你的整个演示应该控制在 3-5 分钟之内。这是一个很重要的技能——用最短的时间清楚地传达你的想法。

建议时间分配：
- 提出问题：30 秒
- 解决方案：30 秒
- 现场演示：1.5-2 分钟（这是最重要的部分！）
- 技术介绍：30 秒
- 未来计划 + 总结：30 秒

在正式演示之前，至少练习 3 次。可以自己对着镜子练，也可以对着同学练。每次练习都计时，确保不超时。 Time Control: 3-5 Minutes

Your entire demo should be 3-5 minutes long. This is an important skill — communicating your ideas clearly in the shortest time possible.

Suggested time allocation:
- The Problem: 30 seconds
- The Solution: 30 seconds
- Live Demo: 1.5-2 minutes (this is the most important part!)
- Tech Stack: 30 seconds
- Future Plans + Wrap-up: 30 seconds

Before the actual presentation, practice at least 3 times. Practice in front of a mirror or with a classmate. Time yourself each time to make sure you don't go over.

准备好你的演示流程：在真正上台之前，把你的演示流程写在纸上。确认你知道在手机上要点击哪些按钮、要演示哪些功能、以及每个步骤要说什么。把它当作一个剧本来准备。 Prepare your demo flow: Before going on stage, write your demo flow on paper. Confirm you know which buttons to tap on your phone, which features to demonstrate, and what to say at each step. Treat it like a script.

10.3 演讲技巧10.3 Presentation Tips

好的演讲不只是内容好，还需要好的表达方式。这里有一些让你的演讲更精彩的技巧： A great presentation isn't just about great content — it also needs great delivery. Here are some tips to make your presentation shine:

用故事或问题开场Open with a Story or Question

不要以"大家好，我是 XXX，我做了一个 App"开始。试试用一个有趣的故事或引人思考的问题开场。

举例：
"你们有没有试过带着宠物去旅行，但不知道哪些酒店允许带宠物？我也遇到过这个问题，所以我做了一个 AI App 来帮助大家解决这个难题！"

这样的开场能立刻抓住观众的注意力。 Don't start with "Hi, I'm XXX, and I made an App." Instead, try opening with an interesting story or thought-provoking question.

Example:
"Have you ever tried traveling with your pet but didn't know which hotels are pet-friendly? I had this problem too, so I built an AI App to help solve it!"

This kind of opening immediately captures the audience's attention.

展示，不要只说Show, Don't Just Tell

现场演示是整个展示中最打动人的部分。不要只是口头描述你的 App 能做什么——直接在手机上展示给大家看！

演示要点：
- 手机屏幕投射到大屏幕上（如果可能的话）
- 操作时解释你正在做什么
- 展示最核心、最酷的功能
- 操作要慢一点，让观众看清楚 The live demo is the most impressive part of your whole presentation. Don't just describe what your App can do — show it on your phone!

Demo tips:
- Mirror your phone screen to a big screen (if possible)
- Explain what you're doing as you operate
- Showcase the most core, coolest features
- Operate slowly so the audience can follow along

清晰自信地表达Speak Clearly & Confidently

演讲时的表达方式和内容一样重要：

- 说话速度：慢一点！紧张时人会不自觉地加速
- 音量：确保最后一排的人也能听到
- 眼神交流：看着观众，不要一直盯着手机
- 肢体语言：站直，面带微笑
- 少用"嗯"和"然后"：需要停顿时，直接暂停就好 How you deliver your presentation is just as important as the content:

- Speaking speed: Slow down! People tend to speed up when nervous
- Volume: Make sure the person in the last row can hear you
- Eye contact: Look at the audience, don't stare at your phone
- Body language: Stand straight, smile
- Avoid filler words: When you need a pause, just pause silently

准备回答问题Prepare for Questions

演示结束后，评委或观众可能会问你问题。提前想想他们可能问什么，准备好答案：

- "你为什么选择这个主题？"
- "这个 App 用了什么 AI 技术？"
- "你遇到的最大困难是什么？"
- "你接下来打算怎么改进？"

不知道答案也没关系，诚实地说"这是个好问题，我还需要进一步研究"就行。 After your demo, judges or audience may ask questions. Think ahead about what they might ask and prepare answers:

- "Why did you choose this topic?"
- "What AI technology does this App use?"
- "What was the biggest challenge you faced?"
- "How do you plan to improve it?"

It's okay if you don't know the answer — just honestly say "That's a great question, I need to research that further."

准备备用方案Have a Backup Plan

现场演示有时候会出意外——Wi-Fi 连不上、API 不响应、App 闪退。专业的演示者永远有备用方案！

备用方案建议：
- 提前录制一段 App 运行的演示视频（30-60 秒）
- 准备几张 App 运行时的截图
- 把截图/视频放在演示文稿（PPT/Google Slides）中

如果现场演示出了问题，你可以平静地说："让我给大家看一段演示视频。"然后播放视频，完全不慌张。 Live demos sometimes go wrong — Wi-Fi won't connect, API doesn't respond, App crashes. Professional presenters always have a backup plan!

Backup plan suggestions:
- Pre-record a demo video of your App running (30-60 seconds)
- Take several screenshots of your App in action
- Put the screenshots/video in a slide deck (PPT/Google Slides)

If the live demo fails, calmly say: "Let me show you a demo video instead." Then play the video — no panic at all.

做好结尾Strong Finish

演示的最后要有一个有力的结尾，而不是说"嗯...就这样吧"。

好的结尾方式：
- 总结你的 App 的核心价值："我的 App 让每个人都能轻松地..."
- 分享你的未来愿景："接下来我计划添加...功能"
- 感谢观众："感谢大家的关注！有任何问题我很乐意回答。"

一个自信的结尾会给评委留下深刻的印象。 End your presentation with a strong finish, not with "Um... that's it I guess."

Good ways to finish:
- Summarize your App's core value: "My App makes it easy for everyone to..."
- Share your future vision: "Next, I plan to add... features"
- Thank the audience: "Thank you for your attention! I'm happy to answer any questions."

A confident ending leaves a strong impression on the judges.

10.4 竞赛提交10.4 Competition Submission

你的 AI App 项目可以提交到 MIT App Inventor Hackathon——这是一个面向全球学生的国际编程竞赛。参加这个竞赛不仅能锻炼你的能力，还能为你的简历和作品集增添一份有分量的成就。 Your AI App project can be submitted to the MIT App Inventor Hackathon — an international coding competition for students worldwide. Participating not only builds your skills, but also adds a valuable achievement to your resume and portfolio.

步骤 1：导出 .aia 文件Step 1: Export the .aia File

在 App Inventor 中，点击 Projects → Export selected project (.aia) to my computer。这会把你的整个项目（包括所有积木块、组件、图片等）打包成一个 .aia 文件下载到你的电脑上。

注意：这个 .aia 文件是你项目的完整备份。把它保存在安全的地方！你随时可以通过 Projects → Import project (.aia) from my computer 把它重新导入到 App Inventor 中。 In App Inventor, click Projects → Export selected project (.aia) to my computer. This packages your entire project (all blocks, components, images, etc.) into a .aia file that downloads to your computer.

Note: This .aia file is a complete backup of your project. Keep it in a safe place! You can re-import it anytime via Projects → Import project (.aia) from my computer.

步骤 2：撰写项目描述Step 2: Write a Project Description

竞赛提交需要一份项目描述文档。用清晰的语言介绍你的项目，包括以下内容：

1. 项目名称：你的 App 叫什么
2. 问题描述：你的 App 解决了什么问题？（2-3 句话）
3. 解决方案：你的 App 是怎么解决这个问题的？（2-3 句话）
4. 使用的技术：MIT App Inventor + 哪些 AI API
5. 使用方法：用户如何使用你的 App？（简单的步骤说明）
6. 未来改进：你还想添加什么功能？

用英文写，因为这是国际竞赛。如果英文不太好，可以先用中文写好，再用 AI 帮你翻译。 Competition submissions require a project description document. Describe your project clearly, including:

1. Project name: What's your App called
2. Problem statement: What problem does your App solve? (2-3 sentences)
3. Solution: How does your App solve this problem? (2-3 sentences)
4. Technologies used: MIT App Inventor + which AI APIs
5. How to use it: How does a user use your App? (simple step-by-step)
6. Future improvements: What features do you want to add?

Write it in English since this is an international competition. If your English isn't strong, write it in Chinese first and use AI to help translate.

步骤 3：录制演示视频Step 3: Record a Demo Video

大多数竞赛都要求提交一段演示视频（通常 1-3 分钟）。视频应该展示你的 App 的核心功能。

录制技巧：
- 使用手机的屏幕录制功能来录制 App 运行画面
- 可以配上你的语音解说（用另一台手机或电脑录音）
- 确保画面清晰稳定，不要抖动
- 先展示最核心的功能，再展示其他功能
- 用免费的视频编辑工具（如 CapCut / iMovie）做简单剪辑

视频结构建议：
- 开头 10 秒：介绍 App 名称和解决的问题
- 中间部分：演示核心功能
- 最后 10 秒：总结和未来计划 Most competitions require a demo video (usually 1-3 minutes). The video should showcase your App's core features.

Recording tips:
- Use your phone's screen recording feature to capture the App in action
- Add voice narration (use another phone or computer to record audio)
- Make sure the video is clear and stable, no shaking
- Show the most core feature first, then other features
- Use free video editing tools (like CapCut / iMovie) for simple edits

Suggested video structure:
- First 10 seconds: Introduce App name and the problem it solves
- Middle section: Demo core features
- Last 10 seconds: Summary and future plans

步骤 4：制作海报或幻灯片Step 4: Create a Poster or Slide Deck

有些竞赛还要求提交一张项目海报或一组幻灯片。海报和幻灯片要简洁有力，用图片和关键词为主，不要放大段文字。

海报/幻灯片应该包括：
- App 名称和图标
- 一句话介绍（你的 App 是做什么的）
- 2-3 张 App 截图
- 使用的技术栈
- 团队成员（如果有的话）

推荐工具：
- Canva：有很多漂亮的海报模板
- Google Slides：免费好用的幻灯片工具 Some competitions also require a project poster or slide deck. Posters and slides should be clean and impactful — use images and keywords, not long paragraphs of text.

Your poster/slides should include:
- App name and icon
- One-line description (what your App does)
- 2-3 App screenshots
- Technology stack used
- Team members (if applicable)

Recommended tools:
- Canva: Has many beautiful poster templates
- Google Slides: Free and easy-to-use slide tool

DSA 评审官看重什么？

2. 技术能力 — 你能使用 App Inventor 和 AI API 这样的工具来创造东西
3. 创造力 — 你的想法是独特的、有趣的，不只是照着教程做
4. 坚持和解决困难 — 你遇到了 Bug 和挑战，但你没有放弃，而是想办法解决了
5. 表达能力 — 你能清楚地向别人解释你做了什么，以及为什么这么做
6. 参赛经历 — 你参加了 MIT App Inventor Hackathon 这样的国际竞赛 What Do DSA Admissions Look For?

1. Real problem-solving ability — Your App isn't a boring exercise; it actually solves a problem
2. Technical skills — You can use tools like App Inventor and AI APIs to create things
3. Creativity — Your idea is unique and interesting, not just following a tutorial
4. Perseverance & problem-solving — You encountered bugs and challenges but didn't give up; you found solutions
5. Communication skills — You can clearly explain what you did and why
6. Competition experience — You participated in international competitions like the MIT App Inventor Hackathon

5. 展示你的 Bug Log——这证明你有系统性解决问题的能力 In your DSA application, you can showcase your AI App project this way:

1. Mention in the application that you completed the AI App Studio course and built an AI App

10.6 课程回顾10.6 Course Review

在过去的 10 节课中，你从零开始学会了使用 App Inventor 制作 AI App。让我们回顾一下你的学习旅程： Over the past 10 lessons, you went from zero to building AI-powered Apps with App Inventor. Let's review your learning journey:

第 1-2 课：App Inventor 基础Lessons 1-2: App Inventor Basics

你学会了 MIT App Inventor 的基本操作——创建项目、添加 UI 组件（按钮、标签、图片）、使用 Blocks 编辑器编写逻辑。你制作了第一个 "Hello AI" App，并学会了在手机上通过 AI Companion 实时测试。你还掌握了变量、条件判断和列表等基础编程概念，开始像一个真正的开发者一样思考问题。 You learned the basics of MIT App Inventor — creating projects, adding UI components (buttons, labels, images), and using the Blocks editor to write logic. You built your first "Hello AI" App and learned to test it on your phone via AI Companion. You also mastered fundamental programming concepts like variables, conditionals, and lists, and started thinking like a real developer.

第 3 课：API 基础Lesson 3: API Fundamentals

你理解了什么是 API（应用程序编程接口）——它就像 App 和外部服务之间的"服务员"。你学会了 URL、请求头（Headers）、请求体（Body）和 API Key 的概念。你用 App Inventor 的 Web 组件发送了你的第一个 API 请求，并成功解析了 JSON 格式的返回数据。这是连接 AI 能力的关键一步！ You understood what an API (Application Programming Interface) is — it's like a "waiter" between your App and external services. You learned about URLs, Headers, Body, and API Keys. You used App Inventor's Web component to send your first API request and successfully parsed JSON response data. This was the critical step to connecting AI capabilities!

第 4-5 课：AI 聊天集成Lessons 4-5: AI Chat Integration

你将 ChatGPT 的 API 集成到了你的 App 中，制作了一个能够和用户对话的 AI 聊天机器人。你学会了如何构建请求体（包括 system prompt 和用户消息）、如何解析 AI 的回复、以及如何管理对话历史来实现多轮对话。你还学习了 Prompt Engineering——通过精心设计提示词来让 AI 给出更好的回答。 You integrated ChatGPT's API into your App, building an AI chatbot that can converse with users. You learned how to construct request bodies (including system prompts and user messages), parse AI responses, and manage conversation history for multi-turn dialogues. You also learned Prompt Engineering — crafting prompts carefully to get better responses from AI.

第 6 课：图像识别Lesson 6: Image Recognition

你让你的 App 拥有了"眼睛"！你学会了使用 Camera 组件拍照、将图片转换为 Base64 编码、并发送给 AI 视觉 API 进行识别。你的 App 可以识别物体、分析场景、甚至读取图片中的文字。你还学到了如何处理图片大小的限制，让 API 调用更高效。 You gave your App "eyes"! You learned to use the Camera component to take photos, convert images to Base64 encoding, and send them to AI Vision APIs for recognition. Your App could identify objects, analyze scenes, and even read text from images. You also learned how to handle image size limitations for more efficient API calls.

第 7 课：语音 AILesson 7: Voice AI

你让你的 App 拥有了"耳朵"和"嘴巴"！你学会了使用 SpeechRecognizer 组件把语音转换为文字（语音识别），以及使用 TextToSpeech 组件让 App 说话（语音合成）。结合之前学的 AI 聊天功能，你制作了一个可以"听懂你说话并用语音回答"的智能助手。 You gave your App "ears" and a "mouth"! You learned to use the SpeechRecognizer component to convert speech to text (speech recognition) and the TextToSpeech component to make your App talk (speech synthesis). Combined with the AI chat features you learned earlier, you built a smart assistant that can "understand your speech and reply with voice."

第 8 课：设计思维Lesson 8: Design Thinking

你学习了设计思维的方法论——从理解用户需求出发，定义要解决的问题，构思创意，制作原型，并进行测试。你为自己的 AI App 项目做了完整的规划，包括确定目标用户、绘制线框图、设计 UI 界面和规划功能列表。你从一个"跟着做"的学生变成了一个"自己设计"的创造者。 You learned the Design Thinking methodology — starting from understanding user needs, defining the problem to solve, brainstorming ideas, creating prototypes, and testing. You made a complete plan for your AI App project, including identifying target users, sketching wireframes, designing UI, and planning a feature list. 课堂测验Lesson Quiz

1. 一个好的项目演示应该按什么顺序来组织？1. What order should a good project demo follow?

一个好的演示应该先提出问题（让观众理解为什么需要这个 App），然后介绍解决方案，接着进行现场演示，再简单介绍技术栈，最后分享未来计划。A good demo should first present the problem (so the audience understands why this App is needed), then introduce the solution, followed by a live demo, a brief tech stack overview, and finally future plans.

2. 为什么在现场演示之前要准备备用方案？2. Why should you prepare a backup plan before a live demo?

现场演示有很多不可控因素（网络、API 服务、设备问题等）。准备一段演示视频或截图作为备用方案，可以确保即使现场出了技术问题，你的展示也能顺利完成。Live demos have many unpredictable factors (network, API service, device issues, etc.). Having a demo video or screenshots as a backup ensures your presentation can go smoothly even if technical problems occur on stage.

3. 在 App Inventor 中，如何导出你的项目文件？3. In App Inventor, how do you export your project file?

在 App Inventor 中，通过 Projects → Export selected project (.aia) to my computer 可以将整个项目导出为 .aia 文件。这个文件包含了所有组件、积木块和资源文件，是项目的完整备份。In App Inventor, you can export your entire project as an .aia file via Projects → Export selected project (.aia) to my computer. This file contains all components, blocks, and resources — a complete backup of your project.

4. DSA 面试中，当面试官问"你遇到的最大困难是什么"时，最佳的回答方式是什么？4. In a DSA interview, what's the best way to answer "What was the biggest challenge you faced?"

最佳的回答方式是描述一个具体的困难（如某个 Bug），然后解释你是怎么分析问题、尝试不同方案、最终解决它的。这展示了你的问题解决能力和成长心态，这正是面试官想看到的。The best approach is to describe a specific challenge (like a particular bug), then explain how you analyzed the problem, tried different approaches, and ultimately solved it. This demonstrates your problem-solving ability and growth mindset — exactly what interviewers want to see.
