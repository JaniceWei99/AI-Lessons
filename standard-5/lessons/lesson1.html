1.2 什么是人工智能？ 1.2 What is Artificial Intelligence?

人工智能（AI）是让机器模拟人类智能的技术——包括感知、推理、学习和决策。简单来说，就是让计算机像人一样"看"、"听"、"想"和"做"。 Artificial Intelligence (AI) is technology that enables machines to simulate human intelligence—perception, reasoning, learning, and decision-making. In simple terms, it's about making computers "see," "hear," "think," and "act" like humans.

🤖 弱 AI（专用 AI） 🤖 Narrow AI (Specialized)

只能做特定任务——下棋、翻译、识别人脸。这就是我们今天使用的所有 AI。 Can only do specific tasks—chess, translation, face recognition. This is all the AI we use today.

  • ✅ 已经存在，广泛使用 ✅ Already exists, widely used
  • ✅ 在特定领域可以超越人类 ✅ Can surpass humans in specific domains
  • ⚠️ 离开训练领域就无能为力 ⚠️ Helpless outside its trained domain

🧠 强 AI（通用 AI） 🧠 General AI (AGI)

能像人一样思考和解决任何问题——目前还不存在！ Can think and solve any problem like humans—doesn't exist yet!

  • ❌ 目前还不存在 ❌ Doesn't exist yet
  • 🔮 科学家们仍在研究 🔮 Scientists are still researching
  • 🎬 常见于科幻电影中 🎬 Commonly seen in sci-fi movies

1.3 AI 发展简史 1.3 A Brief History of AI

人工智能不是一夜之间出现的。让我们沿着时间线，看看 AI 是如何一步步走到今天的。点击卡片查看更多详情！ AI didn't appear overnight. Let's follow the timeline to see how AI got to where it is today. Click each card for more details!

1950

图灵测试 Turing Test

图灵提出"机器能思考吗？" Turing asked "Can machines think?"

阿兰·图灵发表论文，提出了判断机器是否具有智能的标准。如果一台机器能在对话中让人无法区分它和人类，那么它就"通过了"图灵测试。这个想法奠定了人工智能的哲学基础。 Alan Turing published his paper proposing a standard for machine intelligence. If a machine can carry on a conversation indistinguishable from a human, it "passes" the Turing Test. This idea laid the philosophical foundation for AI.

1997

深蓝击败棋王 Deep Blue Beats Kasparov

IBM 的深蓝计算机击败国际象棋世界冠军。 IBM's Deep Blue defeated the world chess champion.

这是 AI 第一次在国际象棋上击败人类顶尖选手，轰动世界。深蓝每秒能评估 2 亿个棋局位置，展示了计算能力在策略游戏中的巨大潜力。 This was the first time AI defeated a top human chess player, stunning the world. Deep Blue could evaluate 200 million positions per second, demonstrating the immense potential of computing power in strategic games.

2012

ImageNet 突破 ImageNet Breakthrough

深度学习在图像识别上取得重大突破。 Deep learning achieved a major breakthrough in image recognition.

AlexNet 将图像识别错误率从 26% 降到 15%，开启了深度学习时代。这次突破证明了深度神经网络在处理视觉任务上的强大能力，引发了 AI 研究的新浪潮。 AlexNet reduced the image recognition error rate from 26% to 15%, ushering in the deep learning era. This breakthrough proved the power of deep neural networks for visual tasks and sparked a new wave of AI research.

2016

AlphaGo 击败李世石 AlphaGo Beats Lee Sedol

AI 在被认为最复杂的棋类游戏围棋中取胜。 AI won in Go, considered the most complex board game.

这一刻被视为 AI 发展的里程碑，因为围棋的可能性比宇宙中的原子还多。AlphaGo 以 4:1 击败韩国棋手李世石，证明了深度强化学习能够解决极其复杂的决策问题。 This moment was seen as a milestone in AI development, because Go has more possible positions than atoms in the universe. AlphaGo defeated Korean player Lee Sedol 4-1, proving that deep reinforcement learning can solve extremely complex decision problems.

2022

ChatGPT 发布 ChatGPT Launch

OpenAI 发布 ChatGPT，引发全球 AI 热潮。 OpenAI released ChatGPT, sparking a global AI craze.

两个月内用户突破 1 亿，成为历史上增长最快的消费应用。ChatGPT 让普通人第一次真正体验到大语言模型的强大能力，从写作、编程到解答问题无所不能。 It reached 100 million users within two months, becoming the fastest-growing consumer application in history. ChatGPT let ordinary people truly experience the power of large language models for the first time—from writing and coding to answering any question.

2024

AI 视频生成 AI Video Generation

Sora 等模型实现文字生成视频。 Models like Sora enabled text-to-video generation.

AI 从文字、图片扩展到视频生成，多模态能力大幅提升。Sora 能根据文字描述生成逼真的视频片段，标志着 AI 创造力进入了新纪元。 AI expanded from text and images to video generation, with a huge leap in multimodal capabilities. Sora can generate realistic video clips from text descriptions, marking a new era in AI creativity.

1.4 AI 的三大能力 1.4 Three Core Capabilities of AI

不管 AI 多么复杂，它的核心能力可以归结为三类： No matter how complex AI gets, its core capabilities boil down to three categories:

感知 Perceive

看、听、读——处理图像、声音和文字 See, hear, read—process images, sound, and text

思考 Think

推理、规划、做决策 Reason, plan, and make decisions

创造 Create

生成文字、图片、代码、音乐 Generate text, images, code, and music

Q1. 以下哪个最准确地描述了人工智能？ Q1. Which of the following best describes artificial intelligence?

AI 是一个广泛的技术领域，核心是让机器模拟人类的感知、推理和学习能力。它不仅是机器人，也不限于超级计算机。 AI is a broad field focused on making machines simulate human perception, reasoning, and learning. It's not limited to robots or supercomputers.

Q2. 目前我们日常生活中的 AI 属于哪种类型？ Q2. What type of AI do we use in daily life currently?

目前所有商用 AI 都是弱 AI（专用 AI），只能完成特定任务。通用 AI（强 AI）目前还不存在。 All commercial AI today is narrow AI, capable of specific tasks only. General AI (strong AI) doesn't exist yet.

Q3. 哪一年 ChatGPT 首次发布？ Q3. What year was ChatGPT first released?

Q4. 以下哪个不是 AI 的核心能力？ Q4. Which of the following is NOT a core capability of AI?

AI 可以模拟情感表达，但不能真正"体验"情感。感知、思考（推理）和创造（生成）是 AI 的三大核心能力。 AI can simulate emotional expression but cannot truly "experience" emotions. Perception, thinking (reasoning), and creation (generation) are AI's three core capabilities.
