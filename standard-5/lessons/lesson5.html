第 5 课 Lesson 5

AI 的未来与责任 The Future & Ethics of AI

思考AI的影响、偏见与安全 Reflect on AI's impact, bias, and safety

5.1 AI 能做什么？不能做什么？ 5.1 What Can AI Do? What Can't It Do?

AI 擅长的 What AI Excels At

  • 模式识别（图像、语音、文字） Pattern recognition (images, speech, text)
  • 快速处理海量数据 Processing massive data quickly
  • 重复性任务自动化 Automating repetitive tasks
  • 7×24小时不间断工作 Working 24/7 without breaks
  • 在特定领域超越人类（围棋、蛋白质折叠） Surpassing humans in specific domains (Go, protein folding)

AI 不擅长的 What AI Struggles With

  • 真正的创造力和原创性 True creativity and originality
  • 常识推理和因果理解 Common sense reasoning and causal understanding
  • 情感理解和同理心 Emotional understanding and empathy
  • 道德判断和价值观 Moral judgment and values
  • 处理全新的、从未见过的情况 Handling completely novel situations
ℹ️

记住：现在的AI都是"弱AI"（专用AI），在特定任务上很强，但没有真正的理解和意识。 Remember: all current AI is "narrow AI"—strong at specific tasks but lacking true understanding or consciousness.

5.2 AI 偏见与公平 5.2 AI Bias & Fairness

AI不会自己产生偏见，但它会从训练数据中"学到"偏见。如果数据有问题，AI的决策也会有问题。 AI doesn't create bias on its own, but it "learns" bias from training data. If data is flawed, AI decisions will be too.

招聘AI歧视女性 Hiring AI Discriminated Against Women

+

亚马逊曾开发AI招聘工具，但发现它系统性地给女性候选人更低的分数。原因：训练数据主要来自过去10年以男性为主的简历。 Amazon developed an AI hiring tool that systematically scored female candidates lower. Reason: training data came from 10 years of mostly male resumes.

人脸识别的种族偏见 Racial Bias in Face Recognition

+

研究发现，一些人脸识别系统对深色皮肤人群的准确率显著低于浅色皮肤人群（错误率相差30%以上）。原因：训练数据中浅色皮肤的照片占多数。 Studies found some face recognition systems had significantly lower accuracy for darker-skinned people (30%+ error rate difference). Reason: training data had mostly lighter-skinned photos.

搜索引擎的刻板印象 Search Engine Stereotypes

+

搜索"CEO"图片时，结果以白人男性为主；搜索"护士"则以女性为主。AI反映并强化了社会中已存在的偏见。 Searching "CEO" images shows mostly white men; "nurse" shows mostly women. AI reflects and reinforces existing social biases.

解决AI偏见需要：多样化的训练数据、公平性审计、人类监督。 Addressing AI bias requires: diverse training data, fairness audits, human oversight.

5.3 深度伪造与信息安全 5.3 Deepfakes & Information Security

Deepfake技术可以生成逼真的假视频、假音频。虽然有娱乐用途，但也被用于欺诈、造谣和冒充他人。 Deepfake technology can generate realistic fake videos and audio. While fun, it's also used for fraud, misinformation, and impersonation.

如何辨别虚假信息 How to Identify Fake Information

  • ✅ 核实来源：信息来自可信的媒体或官方渠道吗？ Verify source: Is the info from a trusted media outlet or official channel?
  • ✅ 检查细节：画面中有没有不自然的闪烁、模糊或变形？ Check details: Are there unnatural flickering, blurring, or distortions?
  • ✅ 交叉验证：其他可靠来源有没有报道同样的事？ Cross-reference: Have other reliable sources reported the same thing?
  • ✅ 使用工具：可以用AI检测工具（如Deepware Scanner）来辨别。 Use tools: AI detection tools (e.g., Deepware Scanner) can help identify fakes.
⚠️

在分享任何令人震惊的内容之前，先想30秒：这是真的吗？ Before sharing any shocking content, think for 30 seconds: Is this real?

5.4 隐私与数据保护 5.4 Privacy & Data Protection

每次你使用AI服务，你的数据可能被收集和使用。了解数据隐私很重要。 Every time you use an AI service, your data may be collected and used. Understanding data privacy matters.

人脸数据 Face Data

人脸解锁、美颜相机、社交媒体照片 Face unlock, beauty cameras, social media photos

对话数据 Conversation Data

和AI助手的聊天记录、搜索历史 Chat logs with AI assistants, search history

行为数据 Behavioral Data

浏览记录、购买习惯、位置信息 Browsing history, purchase habits, location data

思考题 Think About It

你愿意用多少隐私换取多少便利？在使用AI产品时，你会阅读隐私政策吗？ How much privacy are you willing to trade for convenience? Do you read privacy policies when using AI products?

5.5 AI 时代需要什么能力？ 5.5 What Skills Do We Need in the AI Era?

AI不会取代人，但会用AI的人会取代不会用AI的人。 AI won't replace people, but people who use AI will replace those who don't.

批判性思维 Critical Thinking

不盲信AI的输出，能判断信息的真假和质量 Don't blindly trust AI output; judge the truthfulness and quality of information

创造力 Creativity

AI可以辅助创造，但真正的创新灵感来自人类 AI can assist creation, but true innovative inspiration comes from humans

协作能力 Collaboration

学会与AI协作，让AI成为你的得力助手 Learn to collaborate with AI, making it your powerful assistant

终身学习 Lifelong Learning

AI领域发展飞快，持续学习新技能至关重要 AI evolves rapidly; continuously learning new skills is crucial

AI 素养 AI Literacy

理解AI的原理、能力和局限，做负责任的AI使用者 Understand AI's principles, capabilities, and limitations; be a responsible AI user

5.8 结业测验 5.8 Final Quiz

Q1: AI的核心目标是什么？ Q1: What is the core goal of AI?

AI的核心是让机器模拟人类的感知、推理和学习能力，而不是取代人类。 AI's core is simulating human perception, reasoning, and learning, not replacing humans.

Q2: 监督学习和非监督学习的主要区别是什么？ Q2: What's the main difference between supervised and unsupervised learning?

监督学习使用带有"正确答案"标签的数据训练，而非监督学习让AI自己从数据中发现规律。 Supervised learning uses data with "correct answer" labels; unsupervised learning lets AI discover patterns on its own.

Q3: CNN（卷积神经网络）最擅长处理什么？ Q3: What is CNN best at processing?

CNN通过卷积操作提取图像的空间特征，是图像处理的首选架构。 CNNs extract spatial features from images through convolution, making them the go-to architecture for image processing.

Q4: 大语言模型生成文本的基本原理是？ Q4: What is the basic principle of LLM text generation?

LLM通过不断预测下一个最可能的词来生成连贯的文本，本质是一个超强的概率预测模型。 LLMs generate coherent text by continuously predicting the most likely next word—essentially a super-powered probability model.

Q5: 以下哪个不是好的Prompt技巧？ Q5: Which is NOT a good prompt technique?

好的Prompt应该清晰、具体。越模糊的指令越容易得到不符合期望的结果。 Good prompts should be clear and specific. Vague instructions are more likely to produce unexpected results.

Q6: AI偏见的主要来源是什么？ Q6: What is the main source of AI bias?

AI从数据中学习。如果训练数据反映了社会中存在的偏见，AI就会"学到"并放大这些偏见。 AI learns from data. If training data reflects societal biases, AI will "learn" and amplify them.

Q7: 什么是Deepfake？ Q7: What is a Deepfake?

Deepfake利用深度学习技术生成或篡改视频、音频，使其看起来/听起来非常真实。 Deepfakes use deep learning to generate or manipulate video/audio to look/sound very realistic.

Q8: 神经网络中"反向传播"的作用是？ Q8: What is the role of "backpropagation" in neural networks?

反向传播计算每个权重对误差的贡献，并据此调整权重，让模型逐步变得更准确。 Backpropagation calculates each weight's contribution to error and adjusts accordingly, making the model progressively more accurate.

Q9: Transformer架构中的"注意力机制"的作用是？ Q9: What does "attention" in Transformer do?

注意力机制允许模型在处理序列中的每个元素时，动态地确定应该关注哪些其他元素。 Attention allows the model to dynamically determine which other elements to focus on when processing each element in a sequence.

Q10: 在AI时代，以下哪种能力最不重要？ Q10: In the AI era, which ability is least important?

AI可以帮我们存储和检索知识，所以死记硬背变得不那么重要。批判性思维、创造力和AI协作能力才是AI时代的核心竞争力。 AI can store and retrieve knowledge for us, making memorization less important. Critical thinking, creativity, and AI collaboration are the core competencies of the AI era.
