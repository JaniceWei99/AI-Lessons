大语言模型与 AIGC LLMs & AI-Generated Content

揭秘ChatGPT和AI创作的原理 Demystify ChatGPT and AI content generation

4.1 从文字到数字：Token 4.1 From Text to Numbers: Tokens

计算机不认识文字，只认识数字。所以第一步是把文字切成小块（Token），再转成数字。 Computers don't understand text, only numbers. So the first step is to split text into small pieces (tokens), then convert to numbers.

📝

中文示例： Chinese example:

"我喜欢人工智能" → 我 | 喜欢 | 人工 | 智能 → [1024, 3856, 7721, 5539] "我喜欢人工智能" → 我 | 喜欢 | 人工 | 智能 → [1024, 3856, 7721, 5539]

英文示例： English example:

"I love artificial intelligence" → I | love | artificial | intelligence → [40, 1567, 22388, 11478] "I love artificial intelligence" → I | love | artificial | intelligence → [40, 1567, 22388, 11478]

💡

一个Token不一定是一个字或一个词。中文通常1-2个字为一个Token，英文通常3-4个字母为一个Token。GPT-4大约能处理128,000个Token的上下文。 A token isn't necessarily a word. Chinese: usually 1-2 characters per token. English: usually 3-4 letters per token. GPT-4 can handle ~128,000 tokens of context.

4.2 语言模型：预测下一个词 4.2 Language Models: Predicting the Next Word

大语言模型的核心原理其实很简单：给定前面的文字，预测下一个最可能的词。就像手机输入法的联想功能，但强大了亿倍。 The core principle of LLMs is actually simple: given previous text, predict the most likely next word. Like your phone's predictive text, but a billion times more powerful.

预测示例 Prediction Example

"今天天气" → 下一个词的概率： "The weather today is" → Next word probability:

  • 🟩🟩🟩🟩🟩🟩 真好 — 60% 🟩🟩🟩🟩🟩🟩 really nice — 60%
  • 🟩🟩🟩 不错 — 25% 🟩🟩🟩 great — 25%
  • 🟩 很冷 — 10% 🟩 cold — 10%
  • 🟨 晴朗 — 5% 🟨 sunny — 5%

通过不断预测下一个词，一个词接一个词，就能生成完整的文章、代码、诗歌…… By continuously predicting the next word, one after another, it can generate complete articles, code, poems…

4.3 Transformer 简介 4.3 Introduction to Transformer

2017年Google发表了"Attention is All You Need"论文，提出了Transformer架构，这是现代AI的基石。 In 2017, Google published "Attention is All You Need," proposing the Transformer architecture—the cornerstone of modern AI.

注意力机制 Attention Mechanism

想象你在读一段话时，眼睛不会均匀地看每个字，而是会跳到最关键的词。注意力机制让AI也能做到这一点：处理一个词时，它会"关注"句子中最相关的其他词。 When reading a passage, your eyes don't look at every word equally—they jump to the most important ones. Attention lets AI do the same: when processing a word, it "attends to" the most relevant other words.

🔍

例子：在句子"小猫坐在垫子上，它很舒服"中，AI在处理"它"时会特别关注"小猫"，从而理解"它"指的是"小猫"。 Example: In "The cat sat on the mat, it was comfortable," when processing "it," AI pays special attention to "cat," understanding that "it" refers to "cat."

💡

Transformer的名字来源于它能"变换"输入序列的表示方式。GPT中的T就是Transformer！ The name Transformer comes from its ability to "transform" input representations. The T in GPT stands for Transformer!

4.4 ChatGPT 是怎么炼成的 4.4 How ChatGPT is Made

1

预训练 Pre-training

阅读互联网上的海量文本（书籍、网页、论文……），学会语言的基本规律。就像上了12年学。 Read massive amounts of internet text (books, websites, papers…), learning basic language patterns. Like 12 years of school.

2

微调 Fine-tuning

用人类精心准备的对话数据进一步训练，学会以对话的方式回答问题。就像参加了职业培训。 Further trained with human-curated dialogue data, learning to answer in conversational format. Like vocational training.

3

RLHF（人类反馈强化学习） RLHF (Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback)

人类评估AI的回答质量，AI根据反馈不断优化，变得更有用、更安全。就像有了一个导师指导。 Humans evaluate AI response quality, AI continuously optimizes based on feedback, becoming more useful and safe. Like having a mentor.

4.5 Prompt 工程技巧 4.5 Prompt Engineering Tips

好的Prompt能让AI给出更好的回答。这里有5个实用技巧： A good prompt gets better AI responses. Here are 5 practical tips:

1. 明确角色 1. Assign a Role

+

告诉AI它是谁。例如："你是一个资深Python程序员" vs "帮我写代码"。前者会让AI给出更专业、更规范的代码。 Tell AI who it is. E.g., "You are a senior Python programmer" vs "Help me write code." The former gets more professional, well-structured code.

2. 给出示例 2. Provide Examples

+

用示例展示你想要的格式。例如："把下面的句子翻译成英文，格式如下：原文: … 翻译: …" Show the format you want with examples. E.g., "Translate sentences like this: Original: … Translation: …"

3. 分步指令 3. Step-by-step Instructions

+

把复杂任务拆成步骤。例如："第一步分析数据，第二步画图表，第三步写总结"。 Break complex tasks into steps. E.g., "Step 1: analyze data, Step 2: create chart, Step 3: write summary."

4. 限定格式 4. Specify Format

+

明确输出格式。例如："用JSON格式输出"、"用表格形式列出"、"控制在200字以内"。 Define output format. E.g., "Output in JSON format," "List in a table," "Keep under 200 words."

5. 迭代优化 5. Iterate and Refine

+

如果第一次回答不满意，继续追问和修正。AI对话是多轮的，不断优化才能得到最佳结果。 If the first answer isn't satisfactory, keep asking and refining. AI conversation is multi-turn—iterate to get the best results.

4.8 小测验 4.8 Quiz

Q1. 什么是Token？ Q1. What is a Token?

Token是语言模型处理文本的基本单位。文本先被切分成Token，再转换成数字供模型处理。 Tokens are the basic units LLMs use to process text. Text is first split into tokens, then converted to numbers.

Q2. 大语言模型的核心工作原理是什么？ Q2. What is the core working principle of LLMs?

LLM本质上是一个超强的"下一个词预测器"。通过不断预测下一个词，它能生成连贯的文本。 LLMs are essentially super-powered "next word predictors." By continuously predicting the next word, they generate coherent text.

Q3. ChatGPT训练的三个阶段不包括以下哪个？ Q3. Which is NOT one of ChatGPT's three training stages?

ChatGPT的三阶段是：预训练→微调→RLHF。虽然数据清洗很重要，但它不是ChatGPT独特训练流程中的一个阶段。 The three stages are: pre-training→fine-tuning→RLHF. While data cleaning is important, it's not a specific stage of ChatGPT's unique training pipeline.

Q4. 以下哪个是好的Prompt技巧？ Q4. Which is a good prompt technique?

好的Prompt应该包含明确的角色设定、具体的要求、期望的格式等。这样AI才能给出最符合需求的回答。 Good prompts include clear role-setting, specific requirements, expected format, etc.

Q5. Transformer中的"注意力机制"让AI能做什么？ Q5. What does the "Attention mechanism" in Transformers enable AI to do?

注意力机制让模型在处理每个词时能"关注"上下文中最相关的词，理解词与词之间的关系。 Attention lets the model "focus on" the most relevant words in context when processing each word, understanding relationships between words.
