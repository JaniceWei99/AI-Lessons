第 2 课 Lesson 2

什么是人工智能 What Is AI?

AI的定义、弱AI vs 强AI、AI vs 传统软件 Definition of AI, Narrow AI vs General AI, AI vs Traditional Software

2.1 AI 的定义 2.1 Defining AI

人工智能（Artificial Intelligence，AI）是计算机科学的一个分支，致力于创建能够模拟人类智能行为的系统。这包括学习（从数据中获取知识）、推理（根据已知信息得出结论）、感知（理解图像、声音等）和决策（在多种选择中做出判断）。 Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science dedicated to creating systems that can simulate human intelligent behavior. This includes learning (acquiring knowledge from data), reasoning (drawing conclusions from known information), perception (understanding images, sounds, etc.), and decision-making (making judgments among multiple choices).

不同的专家对 AI 有不同的定义。计算机科学之父阿兰·图灵在1950年提出了著名的"图灵测试"：如果一台机器能在对话中让人无法区分它与真人的区别，那么它就可以被认为具有"智能"。 Different experts define AI differently. Alan Turing, the father of computer science, proposed the famous "Turing Test" in 1950: if a machine can carry on a conversation where humans can't distinguish it from a real person, then it can be considered "intelligent."

简单来说，AI 就是让计算机像人一样"看"、"听"、"想"和"做"的技术。 Simply put, AI is the technology that makes computers "see," "hear," "think," and "act" like humans.

2.2 弱 AI vs 强 AI 2.2 Narrow AI vs General AI

AI 按照能力可以分为两大类。理解这个区别非常重要，因为它直接影响我们对 AI 的期望和使用方式。 AI can be divided into two major categories based on capability. Understanding this distinction is crucial, as it directly affects our expectations and how we use AI.

🤖 弱 AI（专用 AI / Narrow AI） 🤖 Narrow AI (Specialized AI)

只擅长完成一项特定任务的 AI。这是今天我们实际使用的所有 AI。 AI that excels at one specific task only. This is all the AI we actually use today.

  • ✅ 已广泛应用：Siri、推荐系统、AlphaGo ✅ Widely used: Siri, recommendations, AlphaGo
  • ✅ 在特定领域能超越人类 ✅ Can surpass humans in specific domains
  • ⚠️ 下棋的 AI 不会开车，翻译的 AI 不会画画 ⚠️ Chess AI can't drive; translation AI can't paint

🧠 强 AI（通用 AI / AGI） 🧠 General AI (AGI)

能像人类一样思考、学习并解决任何类型问题的 AI。目前还不存在！ AI that can think, learn, and solve any type of problem like humans. Doesn't exist yet!

  • ❌ 目前仍是研究目标 ❌ Still a research goal
  • 🔮 何时实现仍有巨大争议 🔮 When it will be achieved is highly debated
  • 🎬 电影中的 AI（如钢铁侠的 JARVIS） 🎬 AI in movies (like Iron Man's JARVIS)

关键要点：不要被科幻电影误导！ChatGPT 虽然很强大，但它仍然是弱 AI——它很擅长处理语言任务，但不能真正"理解"世界，也没有自我意识。 Key takeaway: Don't be misled by sci-fi movies! ChatGPT is powerful, but it's still narrow AI — it's great at language tasks but doesn't truly "understand" the world and has no self-awareness.

2.3 AI vs 传统软件 2.3 AI vs Traditional Software

AI 和普通软件到底有什么不同？最大的区别在于规则从哪里来。 What's really different between AI and regular software? The biggest difference is where the rules come from.

📏 传统软件 📏 Traditional Software

程序员手动编写所有规则。计算机严格按照规则执行。 Programmers manually write all the rules. The computer follows them exactly.

  • 输入 → 固定规则 → 输出 Input → Fixed Rules → Output
  • 例如：计算器（输入 2+3，永远输出 5） Example: Calculator (input 2+3, always outputs 5)
  • 规则越多，代码越复杂 More rules = more complex code

🧠 AI 软件 🧠 AI Software

AI 从数据中自己学习规则。给它大量例子，它自己总结规律。 AI learns rules from data itself. Give it many examples, and it discovers patterns.

  • 数据 + 答案 → AI学习 → 预测新数据 Data + Answers → AI Learning → Predict new data
  • 例如：看1000张猫的图片后，能识别新的猫 Example: After seeing 1000 cat photos, it can recognize new cats
  • 数据越多，表现越好 More data = better performance

打个比方：传统软件像是一本食谱——按照步骤做菜，一步都不能错。AI 更像一个学徒——看师傅做了很多次后，自己也能做出像样的菜，甚至能创造新菜式。 Here's an analogy: Traditional software is like a recipe — follow the steps exactly, no room for error. AI is more like an apprentice — after watching the chef many times, it can cook decent dishes on its own, and even create new ones.

2.4 AI 的主要分支 2.4 Main Branches of AI

人工智能是一个庞大的领域，包含许多子分支。以下是最重要的几个： AI is a vast field with many sub-branches. Here are the most important ones:

机器学习 (ML) Machine Learning (ML)

让计算机从数据中自动学习和改进，不需要明确编程。是当前 AI 最核心的技术。 Enables computers to learn and improve from data automatically without explicit programming. The most core AI technology today.

计算机视觉 (CV) Computer Vision (CV)

让计算机"看懂"图片和视频——人脸识别、物体检测、医学影像分析都属于这个领域。 Enables computers to "understand" images and videos — face recognition, object detection, and medical imaging all belong here.

自然语言处理 (NLP) Natural Language Processing (NLP)

让计算机理解和生成人类语言——翻译、聊天机器人、文本摘要都是 NLP 的应用。 Enables computers to understand and generate human language — translation, chatbots, and text summarization are all NLP applications.

语音识别 Speech Recognition

让计算机"听懂"人说的话——语音助手、语音转文字、声纹识别都依赖这项技术。 Enables computers to "understand" human speech — voice assistants, speech-to-text, and voiceprint recognition all rely on this.

Q1. AI 和传统软件的最大区别是什么？ Q1. What is the biggest difference between AI and traditional software?

传统软件遵循程序员编写的固定规则，而 AI 通过数据自动学习规律——这是两者最根本的区别。 Traditional software follows fixed rules written by programmers, while AI automatically learns patterns from data — this is the most fundamental difference.

Q2. 以下哪个是"弱 AI"的例子？ Q2. Which of the following is an example of "Narrow AI"?

AlphaGo 只能下围棋，不能做其他任务，是典型的弱 AI。JARVIS 和有自我意识的 AI 都属于强 AI（AGI），目前还不存在。 AlphaGo can only play Go and can't do other tasks — a typical narrow AI. JARVIS and self-aware AI are general AI (AGI), which doesn't exist yet.

网页设计是前端开发领域，不属于 AI 的分支。机器学习、计算机视觉和自然语言处理都是 AI 的核心分支。 Web design belongs to front-end development, not AI. Machine learning, computer vision, and NLP are all core branches of AI.
