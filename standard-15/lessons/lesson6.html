第 6 课Lesson 6

深度学习与CNNDeep Learning & CNNs

更深的网络，更强的能力——卷积神经网络如何看见世界？ Deeper networks, stronger abilities — how do CNNs see the world?

6.1 什么是深度学习？6.1 What is Deep Learning?

深度学习就是使用很多层隐藏层的神经网络。"深度"指的是网络层数多——可以有几十甚至上百层！层数越多，网络就能学习越复杂的特征和规律。 Deep learning uses neural networks with many hidden layers. "Deep" refers to the number of layers — there can be dozens or even hundreds! More layers enable the network to learn increasingly complex features and patterns.

浅层网络（1-2层）Shallow Network (1-2 layers)

  • 只能学简单的规律Can only learn simple patterns
  • 参数少，训练快Few parameters, fast training
  • 适合简单任务Good for simple tasks

深度网络（多层）Deep Network (Many layers)

  • 能学习非常复杂的规律Can learn very complex patterns
  • 参数多，需要大量数据和算力Many parameters, needs lots of data & compute
  • 在图像、语音、文本上表现出色Excels at images, speech, and text

6.2 卷积神经网络（CNN）6.2 Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN)

CNN 是专门为处理图像设计的深度学习网络。它模仿人眼看东西的方式——先看局部细节，再组合成整体。 CNNs are deep learning networks specifically designed for images. They mimic how human eyes see — first looking at local details, then combining them into a whole picture.

卷积层Convolution Layer

用小"滤波器"在图片上滑动，提取边缘、纹理等特征。就像用放大镜一小块一小块地检查图片。Slides small "filters" across the image, extracting edges, textures, etc. Like using a magnifying glass to examine the image piece by piece.

池化层Pooling Layer

缩小图片尺寸但保留重要信息。就像画缩略图——保留关键特征，去掉不重要的细节。Reduces image size while keeping important info. Like making a thumbnail — keep key features, remove unimportant details.

全连接层Fully Connected Layer

把提取的特征综合起来做最终决策。就像把所有线索汇总，给出"这是猫"的最终判断。Combines extracted features for final decision. Like gathering all clues to make the final judgment "this is a cat."

6.3 CNN 如何"看"图片6.3 How CNNs "See" Images

CNN 分层提取特征，从简单到复杂，就像人眼识别物体的过程： CNNs extract features layer by layer, from simple to complex, like how human eyes recognize objects:

Layer 1

边缘检测Edge Detection

第一层学会识别简单的边缘和线条——水平线、垂直线、斜线。这是最基础的视觉特征。The first layer learns to detect simple edges and lines — horizontal, vertical, diagonal. These are the most basic visual features.

Layer 2-3

纹理和形状Textures & Shapes

中间层把边缘组合成更复杂的特征：眼睛的形状、毛发的纹理、耳朵的轮廓。Middle layers combine edges into more complex features: eye shapes, fur textures, ear outlines.

Layer 4+

物体识别Object Recognition

深层把形状组合成完整的物体：圆耳朵 + 胡须 + 尖鼻子 = 猫！做出最终的识别判断。Deep layers combine shapes into complete objects: round ears + whiskers + pointed nose = cat! Making the final recognition judgment.

6.4 著名的 CNN 架构6.4 Famous CNN Architectures

CNN 的发展历程中出现了很多里程碑式的架构： Many milestone architectures have appeared in CNN's history:

LeNet（1998）LeNet (1998)

CNN的"始祖"，由Yann LeCun提出，最早用于手写数字识别。只有5层，但开创了CNN的先河。The "ancestor" of CNNs, proposed by Yann LeCun, first used for handwritten digit recognition. Only 5 layers, but pioneered CNN.

AlexNet（2012）AlexNet (2012)

ImageNet竞赛冠军，将图像识别错误率从26%降到15%，引爆了深度学习革命。ImageNet competition winner, cut image recognition error from 26% to 15%, sparking the deep learning revolution.

ResNet（2015）ResNet (2015)

152层的超深网络！引入"跳跃连接"，在ImageNet上首次超越人类水平（错误率3.6%，人类5.1%）。A 152-layer super-deep network! Introduced "skip connections," first surpassed human-level on ImageNet (3.6% error vs human 5.1%).

EfficientNet（2019）EfficientNet (2019)

追求效率的CNN。同样准确度下参数更少，证明了"更聪明的设计"比"更大"更重要。Efficiency-focused CNN. Fewer parameters for same accuracy, proving "smarter design" matters more than "bigger."

6.5 深度学习的GPU革命6.5 The GPU Revolution in Deep Learning

深度学习为什么现在才爆发？一个关键原因是GPU（图形处理器）。它本来是为游戏设计的，没想到成了AI训练的神器！ Why did deep learning only explode now? A key reason is the GPU. Originally designed for gaming, it became an AI training powerhouse!

CPU 训练CPU Training

像一个超级聪明的厨师，一次只能做一道菜，但每道菜做得又快又好。Like one super-smart chef who can only cook one dish at a time, but does each perfectly.

GPU 训练GPU Training

像1000个普通厨师同时做菜。虽然每人没那么精，但1000道菜同时出锅！深度学习正好需要大量并行计算。Like 1,000 average chefs cooking simultaneously. Each isn't as skilled, but 1,000 dishes come out at once! Deep learning needs massive parallel computation.

你知道吗？训练一个大型深度学习模型可能需要几百块GPU运行几周，电费可能超过100万元！Did you know? Training a large deep learning model might need hundreds of GPUs running for weeks, with electricity costs over $100,000!

小测验Quiz

Q1. "深度学习"中的"深度"指的是什么？Q1. What does "deep" in "deep learning" refer to?

"深度"指神经网络有很多隐藏层（几十到上百层），让网络能学习更复杂的特征和规律。"Deep" means the neural network has many hidden layers (dozens to hundreds), enabling more complex feature and pattern learning.

Q2. CNN 中卷积层的作用是什么？Q2. What does the convolution layer do?

卷积层使用小的滤波器在图像上滑动，提取边缘、纹理等局部特征。Convolution layers slide small filters across images to extract local features like edges and textures.

Q3. 哪个CNN在ImageNet上首次超越人类水平？Q3. Which CNN first surpassed human-level on ImageNet?

ResNet（2015）在ImageNet上实现了3.6%的错误率，首次低于人类的5.1%错误率。ResNet (2015) achieved 3.6% error on ImageNet, first beating the human error rate of 5.1%.

Q4. 为什么GPU比CPU更适合深度学习训练？Q4. Why are GPUs better than CPUs for deep learning?

GPU有数千个小核心，可以同时执行大量简单计算，非常适合深度学习中的矩阵运算。GPUs have thousands of small cores for many simultaneous calculations, ideal for matrix operations in deep learning.

Q5. CNN的第一层通常学习到什么类型的特征？Q5. What features does the first CNN layer typically learn?

CNN第一层学习最基础的特征：边缘和线条。更深的层逐步学习纹理、形状，最后识别完整物体。CNN's first layer learns basic features: edges and lines. Deeper layers progressively learn textures, shapes, and finally complete objects.
