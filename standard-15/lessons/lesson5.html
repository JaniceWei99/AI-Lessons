第 5 课Lesson 5

监督学习Supervised Learning

给 AI 带上"老师"——用标注好的数据教它学习Giving AI a "teacher" — training it with labeled data

5.1 什么是监督学习？5.1 What Is Supervised Learning?

监督学习（Supervised Learning）是机器学习中最常用的方法。"监督"的意思是有老师——在这里，"老师"就是带有标签（正确答案）的数据。Supervised Learning is the most commonly used method in machine learning. "Supervised" means there's a teacher — here, the "teacher" is data with labels (correct answers).

就像学生做练习题时有参考答案一样，AI 通过对比自己的预测和正确答案，不断纠正自己的错误，逐渐变得越来越准确。Just like students doing practice problems with answer keys, AI compares its predictions with correct answers, constantly correcting its mistakes, and gradually becoming more accurate.

监督学习的过程可以概括为三步：The supervised learning process can be summarized in three steps:

1. 输入数据1. Input Data

把带标签的训练数据（如猫/狗图片+标签）输入模型Feed labeled training data (e.g., cat/dog images + labels) into the model

2. 学习规律2. Learn Patterns

模型对比预测与标签的差异，自动调整内部参数来减少误差Model compares predictions vs labels, auto-adjusting parameters to reduce errors

3. 预测新数据3. Predict New Data

训练完成后，模型就能对从未见过的新数据做出准确预测After training, the model can accurately predict unseen new data

5.2 分类：这是猫还是狗？5.2 Classification: Cat or Dog?

监督学习最常见的任务之一是分类（Classification）——把数据归入不同的类别。One of the most common supervised learning tasks is classification — assigning data to different categories.

二分类Binary Classification

只有两个类别。例如：邮件是"垃圾邮件"还是"正常邮件"？评论是"积极"还是"消极"？照片中是"猫"还是"狗"？Only two categories. Examples: Is an email "spam" or "not spam"? Is a review "positive" or "negative"? Is the photo a "cat" or "dog"?

多分类Multi-class Classification

多个类别。例如：这张图片是"猫"、"狗"、"鸟"还是"鱼"？这首歌是"流行"、"摇滚"、"古典"还是"嘻哈"？Multiple categories. Examples: Is this image a "cat," "dog," "bird," or "fish"? Is this song "pop," "rock," "classical," or "hip-hop"?

生活中的分类：你的手机相册自动将照片按"人物"、"风景"、"美食"分类，这就是 AI 分类在起作用。社交媒体检测和过滤不当言论，也是文本分类的应用。Classification in real life: Your phone gallery automatically sorts photos into "people," "scenery," "food" — that's AI classification at work. Social media detecting and filtering inappropriate comments is also a text classification application.

5.3 回归：预测一个数字5.3 Regression: Predicting a Number

分类是预测类别（离散的），而回归（Regression）是预测一个连续的数值。Classification predicts categories (discrete), while regression predicts a continuous numerical value.

房价预测House Price Prediction

根据面积、地段、楼层等特征，预测房子的价格（比如 350 万元）Based on area, location, floor, etc., predict house price (e.g., $350,000)

温度预测Temperature Prediction

根据历史气象数据，预测明天的气温（比如 28.5°C）Based on historical weather data, predict tomorrow's temperature (e.g., 28.5°C)

股票预测Stock Prediction

根据市场数据，预测股票的价格走势（注意：AI 也不能保证准确！）Based on market data, predict stock price trends (Note: AI can't guarantee accuracy either!)

分类 vs 回归的关键区别：分类的输出是类别（"猫"或"狗"），回归的输出是数字（28.5°C 或 350万元）。记住这个区别，你就掌握了监督学习的两大核心任务！Key difference between classification and regression: Classification outputs categories ("cat" or "dog"), regression outputs numbers (28.5°C or $350,000). Remember this distinction and you've mastered the two core tasks of supervised learning!

5.4 过拟合与欠拟合5.4 Overfitting & Underfitting

AI 的学习也可能"出问题"。最常见的两种情况是过拟合和欠拟合。AI's learning can also go wrong. The two most common problems are overfitting and underfitting.

📖 过拟合 (Overfitting)📖 Overfitting

AI "死记硬背"了训练数据，在训练集上表现完美，但面对新数据就"翻车"。AI "memorized" the training data, performs perfectly on training set but fails on new data.

  • 就像一个学生只背答案，不理解原理Like a student who only memorizes answers without understanding
  • 训练成绩 99%，测试成绩 50%Training score 99%, test score 50%

💤 欠拟合 (Underfitting)💤 Underfitting

AI 连训练数据都没学好，在哪里都表现很差。AI hasn't even learned the training data well, performing poorly everywhere.

  • 就像一个完全没复习的学生Like a student who didn't study at all
  • 训练成绩 50%，测试成绩 45%Training score 50%, test score 45%

理想状态：AI 在训练集和测试集上都表现良好——这说明它真正"学会"了，而不是死记硬背。就像一个学生真正理解了知识，无论遇到什么样的考题都能灵活应对。Ideal state: AI performs well on both training and test sets — this means it truly "learned," not just memorized. Like a student who truly understood the material and can handle any exam question flexibly.
