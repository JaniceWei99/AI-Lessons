计算机视觉Computer Vision

教机器"看"世界——从人脸识别到自动驾驶的视觉AI Teaching machines to "see" — from face recognition to self-driving visual AI

11.1 什么是计算机视觉？11.1 What is Computer Vision?

计算机视觉（CV）让机器能像人类一样"看"并理解图像和视频。但对机器来说，一张图片只是一堆数字——每个像素都是一个RGB颜色值。CV的任务就是从这些数字中提取出有意义的信息。 Computer Vision (CV) enables machines to "see" and understand images and videos like humans. But to machines, an image is just a bunch of numbers — each pixel is an RGB color value. CV's job is extracting meaningful information from these numbers.

想象一下：一张1080p的照片包含约200万个像素，每个像素有红绿蓝三个值。计算机看到的就是600万个数字！它需要从这些数字中理解"这是一只正在奔跑的金毛犬"。Imagine: A 1080p photo contains about 2 million pixels, each with red, green, blue values. The computer sees 6 million numbers! It needs to understand from these numbers that "this is a golden retriever running."

11.2 CV 的核心任务11.2 Core CV Tasks

图像分类Image Classification

告诉你图片里是什么："这是猫"、"这是飞机"。整张图片给出一个分类标签。是CV最基础的任务。Tells you what's in the image: "this is a cat," "this is a plane." Assigns one label to the whole image. The most basic CV task.

目标检测Object Detection

找到图片中所有物体的位置并识别。用方框标出每个物体："这里有一只猫，那里有一条狗"。自动驾驶和安防监控的核心技术。Find and identify all objects with bounding boxes: "here's a cat, there's a dog." Core tech for self-driving and security surveillance.

语义分割Semantic Segmentation

精确到每个像素！标出图片中每个像素属于什么类别：天空、道路、建筑、行人。医学影像分析用这个来找出肿瘤的精确边界。Pixel-level precision! Label each pixel's category: sky, road, building, pedestrian. Medical imaging uses this to find exact tumor boundaries.

姿态估计Pose Estimation

识别人体关节的位置：头、肩、肘、手、膝等。用于运动分析、舞蹈教学、体感游戏和手语识别。Detect body joint positions: head, shoulders, elbows, hands, knees. Used in sports analysis, dance teaching, motion games, and sign language recognition.

11.3 人脸识别技术11.3 Face Recognition Technology

人脸识别是计算机视觉中最广泛应用的技术之一。你每天用手机面部解锁就在使用这项技术。 Face recognition is one of CV's most widely used technologies. You use it daily when unlocking your phone with your face.

Step 1

人脸检测Face Detection

从照片或视频中找到所有人脸的位置。即使照片中有很多人，AI也能准确找到每张脸。Find all faces in a photo or video. Even in crowded scenes, AI can accurately locate every face.

Step 2

特征提取Feature Extraction

分析脸部的关键特征：眼距、鼻子形状、嘴巴宽度等。把每张脸变成一组独特的数字特征（"脸部指纹"）。Analyze facial features: eye distance, nose shape, mouth width. Convert each face to unique numerical features (a "facial fingerprint").

Step 3

比对匹配Compare & Match

把提取的特征与数据库中的已知人脸进行比对。如果特征足够相似，就判断是同一个人。Compare extracted features against known faces in a database. If features are similar enough, it's identified as the same person.

11.4 自动驾驶中的视觉AI11.4 Visual AI in Self-Driving

自动驾驶汽车是计算机视觉最复杂的应用之一。车辆需要实时"看懂"周围环境： Self-driving cars are among CV's most complex applications. Vehicles must "understand" their surroundings in real-time:

识别交通标志Recognize Signs

限速标志、停车标志、红绿灯——AI必须在毫秒内准确识别并做出反应。Speed limits, stop signs, traffic lights — AI must recognize and react in milliseconds.

检测行人和车辆Detect Pedestrians & Cars

实时检测周围所有移动物体，预测它们的运动轨迹，避免碰撞。Real-time detection of all moving objects, predicting their trajectories to avoid collisions.

理解车道线Understand Lane Lines

识别车道标记，即使是褪色或部分遮挡的。确保汽车保持在正确的车道内。Identify lane markings, even faded or partially obscured ones. Ensure the car stays in the correct lane.

挑战：自动驾驶的CV系统必须在雨天、雾天、夜间等恶劣条件下也能正常工作。这比人类驾驶员面临的挑战更大，因为摄像头不像人眼那样适应性强。Challenge: Self-driving CV systems must work in rain, fog, and night conditions. This is harder than human driving, because cameras aren't as adaptive as human eyes.

11.5 CV 在生活中的应用11.5 CV in Everyday Life

医学影像Medical Imaging

AI分析X光片、CT扫描、MRI图像，帮助医生更快更准确地发现疾病。有些AI检测某些癌症的准确率已经超过了人类医生。AI analyzes X-rays, CT scans, MRI images, helping doctors detect diseases faster and more accurately. Some AI cancer detection accuracy exceeds human doctors.

AR 滤镜AR Filters

抖音和Instagram的特效滤镜——AI实时检测你的脸部特征，精确地叠加上猫耳朵、兔子鼻子等可爱效果。TikTok and Instagram effect filters — AI detects your facial features in real-time, precisely overlaying cat ears, bunny noses, and cute effects.

OCR 文字识别OCR Text Recognition

扫描纸质文件自动转文字。从古老的手写信到路牌上的文字，AI都能快速识别。翻译App的"拍照翻译"就用了这个技术。Scan paper documents to text automatically. From old handwritten letters to road signs, AI recognizes text quickly. Translation apps' "photo translate" uses this.

智能安防Smart Security

商场、机场的监控系统用CV自动检测异常行为：有人摔倒、遗留可疑包裹、拥挤踩踏风险等。Mall and airport surveillance uses CV to auto-detect anomalies: someone falling, suspicious packages, crowd crush risks, etc.

小测验Quiz

Q1. 计算机"看"到的图片本质上是什么？Q1. What does a computer fundamentally "see" in an image?

计算机看到的图片就是一堆数字——每个像素由红(R)、绿(G)、蓝(B)三个数值组成。计算机视觉的任务是从这些数字中提取有意义的信息。Computers see images as numbers — each pixel has Red, Green, Blue values. Computer vision's task is extracting meaningful information from these numbers.

Q2. 哪个CV任务能精确到每个像素？Q2. Which CV task works at pixel-level precision?

语义分割标注图片中每一个像素属于什么类别，精确度最高。目标检测使用方框，图像分类只给出整体标签。Semantic segmentation labels every pixel's category — highest precision. Object detection uses boxes; classification gives only a whole-image label.

Q3. 人脸识别的第二步是什么？Q3. What is the second step in face recognition?

人脸识别三步：1)检测人脸位置 2)提取脸部特征（生成"脸部指纹"） 3)与数据库比对匹配。Face recognition: 1) Detect face location 2) Extract facial features ("facial fingerprint") 3) Compare with database.

Q4. 自动驾驶视觉系统面临的最大挑战之一是什么？Q4. What is one major challenge for self-driving vision systems?

雨天、雾天、夜间等恶劣条件下，摄像头的视野受限，对CV系统的准确性是巨大挑战。In rain, fog, and night conditions, camera visibility is limited, posing huge challenges to CV system accuracy.

Q5. 以下哪个不是计算机视觉的日常应用？Q5. Which is NOT a daily computer vision application?

天气预报主要依赖气象数据和数值模拟，不属于计算机视觉的典型应用。面部解锁、拍照翻译、AR滤镜都是CV技术。Weather forecasting mainly relies on meteorological data and numerical simulations, not a typical CV application. Face unlock, photo translation, and AR filters all use CV.
