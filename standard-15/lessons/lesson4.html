第 4 课Lesson 4

数据：AI 的粮食Data: AI's Food

没有数据，AI 就是一个"饿肚子"的学生——什么也学不会Without data, AI is a "starving" student — it can't learn anything

4.1 什么是数据集？4.1 What Is a Dataset?

要训练 AI，首先需要大量的数据。这些数据的集合叫做数据集（Dataset）。你可以把数据集想象成一本教科书——AI 就是从这本"教科书"中学习知识的。To train AI, you first need lots of data. A collection of this data is called a dataset. Think of a dataset as a textbook — AI learns its knowledge from this "textbook."

比如，要教 AI 识别猫和狗，你需要准备一个包含成千上万张猫和狗图片的数据集。每张图片就是一个数据样本。数据集越大、越多样化，AI 学得就越好。For example, to teach AI to recognize cats and dogs, you need a dataset containing thousands of cat and dog images. Each image is a data sample. The larger and more diverse the dataset, the better AI learns.

图像数据集Image Datasets

ImageNet 包含超过 1400 万张图片，涵盖 2 万多个类别。它是推动计算机视觉发展的关键数据集。ImageNet contains over 14 million images across 20,000+ categories. It's a key dataset that drove computer vision progress.

文本数据集Text Datasets

训练 ChatGPT 的数据集包含了互联网上数十亿个网页的文本内容，相当于数百万本书的信息量。The dataset used to train ChatGPT contains text from billions of web pages, equivalent to millions of books worth of information.

4.2 标签：给数据贴"名牌"4.2 Labels: Putting "Name Tags" on Data

光有数据还不够——AI 还需要知道每个数据样本的正确答案。这个正确答案就叫做标签（Label）。Data alone isn't enough — AI also needs to know the correct answer for each data sample. This correct answer is called a label.

比如，你给 AI 看一张猫的图片，同时告诉它"这是猫"。这个"猫"就是这张图片的标签。通过大量带标签的数据，AI 就能学会自己判断新的图片是猫还是狗。For example, you show AI a cat picture and tell it "this is a cat." The word "cat" is the label for that image. Through many labeled examples, AI learns to judge whether new images are cats or dogs on its own.

🏷️ 有标签的数据🏷️ Labeled Data

每个数据样本都附带了正确答案。Each data sample comes with the correct answer.

  • 图片 → "这是猫" / "这是狗"Image → "This is a cat" / "This is a dog"
  • 邮件 → "垃圾邮件" / "正常邮件"Email → "Spam" / "Not spam"
  • 用于监督学习Used for supervised learning

📦 无标签的数据📦 Unlabeled Data

只有原始数据，没有标注答案。Only raw data, no annotated answers.

  • 一堆没有分类的图片A bunch of uncategorized images
  • 互联网上的海量文本Massive text from the internet
  • 用于无监督学习Used for unsupervised learning

趣味知识：给数据打标签是一项巨大的工程！ImageNet 数据集的标注工作动用了来自 167 个国家的近 5 万名工人，通过众包平台完成。这就是为什么高质量的标注数据如此珍贵。Fun fact: Labeling data is a massive undertaking! ImageNet's labeling work employed nearly 50,000 workers from 167 countries through crowdsourcing platforms. That's why high-quality labeled data is so precious.

4.3 训练集与测试集4.3 Training Set & Test Set

想象你正在准备期末考试。你会用课本和习题来学习（训练），然后用模拟考试来检验自己学得怎么样（测试）。AI 的学习过程也是一样的！Imagine you're preparing for a final exam. You use textbooks and practice problems to study (train), then use a mock exam to test how well you learned (test). AI's learning process works the same way!

📚 训练集 (Training Set)📚 Training Set

AI 用来学习的数据。通常占总数据的 70-80%。Data AI uses to learn. Usually 70-80% of total data.

  • 就像你的课本和练习题Like your textbook and practice problems
  • AI 反复学习这些数据，调整自己的"参数"AI studies this data repeatedly, adjusting its "parameters"

📝 测试集 (Test Set)📝 Test Set

AI 用来考试的数据。通常占总数据的 20-30%。Data used to test AI. Usually 20-30% of total data.

  • 就像你的模拟考试Like your mock exam
  • AI 从未见过这些数据，用来检验真实水平AI has never seen this data — it tests real ability

为什么要分开？如果你用考试原题来复习，当然能考满分——但你并没有真正学会。同样，如果 AI 用测试集来训练，它的成绩会虚高，在面对新数据时就会"翻车"。这叫做过拟合。Why split them? If you study using the exact exam questions, you'd score perfectly — but you haven't truly learned. Similarly, if AI trains on its test set, its scores would be inflated, and it would fail on new data. This is called overfitting.

4.4 数据质量：垃圾进，垃圾出4.4 Data Quality: Garbage In, Garbage Out

在 AI 领域有一句名言："Garbage In, Garbage Out"（垃圾进，垃圾出）。意思是，如果你给 AI 喂的数据质量很差，那它学到的东西也一定很差。There's a famous saying in AI: "Garbage In, Garbage Out" (GIGO). It means if you feed AI poor quality data, what it learns will also be poor.

数据偏差Data Bias

如果训练数据只包含白人面孔，AI 识别其他肤色的人脸时准确率会大幅下降。数据必须具有代表性。If training data only includes white faces, AI's accuracy drops significantly for other skin tones. Data must be representative.

标签错误Label Errors

如果把一张狗的图片标记为"猫"，AI 就会学到错误的知识。标签的准确性直接影响模型质量。If a dog image is labeled as "cat," AI learns the wrong thing. Label accuracy directly affects model quality.

数据量不足Insufficient Data

数据太少就像课本只有几页——AI 没有足够的例子来归纳出正确的规律。Too little data is like a textbook with only a few pages — AI doesn't have enough examples to find the right patterns.

数据噪声Data Noise

模糊的图片、错误的信息都是"噪声"。过多噪声会干扰 AI 的学习，就像在嘈杂环境中很难听清老师讲课。Blurry images, incorrect info are "noise." Too much noise disrupts learning, like trying to hear a teacher in a noisy room.

4.5 小测验4.5 Quiz

让我们来检验一下你在本课中学到的知识！Let's test what you've learned in this lesson!

Q1. 在机器学习中，"标签"（Label）指的是什么？Q1. In machine learning, what does "label" refer to?

标签是人为给数据标注的正确答案。比如告诉 AI 一张图片是"猫"还是"狗"，这个分类就是标签。A label is the correct answer manually annotated for data. For example, telling AI an image is a "cat" or "dog" — this classification is the label.

Q2. 为什么需要把数据分成训练集和测试集？Q2. Why do we split data into training and test sets?

如果用训练过的数据来测试，AI 的成绩会虚高（过拟合）。测试集中的数据是 AI 从未见过的，才能真实反映它的能力。If we test with training data, AI's scores would be inflated (overfitting). Test set data is unseen by AI, giving a true measure of its ability.

Q3. "Garbage In, Garbage Out" 这句话强调了什么？Q3. What does "Garbage In, Garbage Out" emphasize?

"垃圾进，垃圾出"意味着如果输入的数据质量很差，AI 学到的东西也必然很差。数据质量是 AI 的根基。"Garbage In, Garbage Out" means if input data is poor quality, what AI learns will also be poor. Data quality is AI's foundation.
