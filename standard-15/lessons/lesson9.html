第 9 课Lesson 9

Prompt 工程与应用Prompt Engineering & Applications

掌握与AI对话的艺术——学会写出更好的提示词！ Master the art of talking to AI — learn to write better prompts!

9.1 什么是 Prompt？9.1 What is a Prompt?

Prompt（提示词）就是你给AI的指令或问题。就像跟一个很聪明但很"实在"的朋友说话——你问得越清楚、越具体，得到的答案就越好。Prompt 工程就是研究如何写出更有效提示词的技术。 A prompt is the instruction or question you give to AI. It's like talking to a very smart but very "literal" friend — the clearer and more specific your question, the better the answer. Prompt engineering is the skill of writing more effective prompts.

差的提示词Poor Prompt

"帮我写个东西""Write me something"

太模糊了！AI不知道你想要什么类型、什么主题、多长、什么风格的内容。Too vague! AI doesn't know what type, topic, length, or style you want.

好的提示词Good Prompt

"请用轻松幽默的口吻，为13岁学生写一篇300字的科普文章，主题是'为什么天空是蓝色的'""In a fun, humorous tone, write a 300-word science article for 13-year-olds about 'why the sky is blue'"

明确了受众、字数、风格和主题。AI可以精准地给出你想要的内容。Specifies audience, length, tone, and topic. AI can deliver exactly what you want.

9.2 Prompt 写作的黄金法则9.2 Golden Rules of Prompt Writing

记住这些法则，你的AI对话质量会大幅提升： Remember these rules to dramatically improve your AI conversations:

1. 明确角色1. Define the Role

"你是一位经验丰富的数学老师"——给AI一个身份，它会用那个角色的专业知识和语言风格来回答。"You are an experienced math teacher" — giving AI an identity makes it respond with that role's expertise and language style.

2. 具体明确2. Be Specific

说清楚你想要什么格式（列表/表格/段落）、多长、什么风格。越具体越好。State the format (list/table/paragraph), length, and style you want. The more specific, the better.

3. 给出示例3. Provide Examples

"按照这个格式回答：[示例]"——给AI一个参考样本，它能更准确地理解你的需求。"Answer in this format: [example]" — giving AI a sample helps it understand your needs more accurately.

4. 分步提问4. Ask Step by Step

复杂问题拆成小步骤："先分析问题，然后列出解决方案，最后推荐最佳方案并说明理由。"Break complex questions into steps: "First analyze the problem, then list solutions, finally recommend the best one with reasons."

9.3 高级 Prompt 技巧9.3 Advanced Prompt Techniques

掌握了基础后，来学习一些进阶技巧： After mastering the basics, learn some advanced techniques:

零样本提示Zero-Shot Prompting

直接给任务，不提供任何示例。"把这段英文翻译成中文：Hello World"。适合简单任务。Give the task directly without examples. "Translate to Chinese: Hello World." Good for simple tasks.

少样本提示Few-Shot Prompting

先给几个示例，再让AI完成任务。"开心→😊，伤心→😢，生气→？"AI就会理解模式并回答"😡"。Give a few examples first, then the task. "Happy→😊, Sad→😢, Angry→?" AI understands the pattern and answers "😡".

思维链（CoT）Chain of Thought (CoT)

"请一步一步地思考"——这句神奇的话能让AI像人一样分步推理，大幅提高复杂问题的准确率。"Think step by step" — this magical phrase makes AI reason step-by-step like humans, greatly improving accuracy on complex problems.

试一试：问AI一道数学题时加上"请一步一步思考"，对比不加这句话的回答，你会发现准确率显著提升！Try it: When asking AI a math problem, add "think step by step" and compare with answers without it. You'll see a significant accuracy improvement!

9.4 实用 Prompt 模板9.4 Practical Prompt Templates

这些模板可以直接使用，帮你更好地跟AI合作： These templates can be used directly to help you work better with AI:

学习助手Study Helper

"你是一位耐心的[学科]老师。我是初中生，请用简单易懂的语言解释[概念]，并举一个生活中的例子。""You are a patient [subject] teacher. I'm a middle school student. Please explain [concept] in simple language with a real-life example."

写作助手Writing Helper

"请帮我写一篇[类型]文章，主题是[话题]，字数[N]字左右，风格[正式/幽默/抒情]，目标读者是[描述]。""Write a [type] article about [topic], roughly [N] words, in [formal/humorous/lyrical] style, for [audience description]."

代码助手Code Helper

"用[编程语言]写一个[功能描述]的程序。要求：1)代码要有注释；2)包含错误处理；3)给出运行示例。""Write a [function description] program in [language]. Requirements: 1) Add comments 2) Include error handling 3) Show example output."

辩论助手Debate Helper

"关于[话题]，请分别从支持方和反对方的角度，各列出3个论点及论据，然后给出你的综合分析。""About [topic], list 3 arguments with evidence from both supporting and opposing sides, then give your balanced analysis."

9.5 Prompt 的注意事项9.5 Prompt Dos and Don'ts

使用AI时，有一些重要的注意事项： When using AI, keep these important considerations in mind:

应该做Do

  • 核实AI给出的事实和数据Verify facts and data from AI
  • 迭代优化你的提示词Iteratively refine your prompts
  • 保护个人隐私信息Protect personal privacy
  • 用AI辅助学习，而非替代思考Use AI to assist learning, not replace thinking

不应该做Don't

  • 把AI的回答直接当作业交Submit AI answers directly as homework
  • 向AI透露密码等敏感信息Share passwords or sensitive info with AI
  • 完全依赖AI而不自己思考Rely entirely on AI without thinking yourself
  • 用AI来做违法或不道德的事Use AI for illegal or unethical purposes

小测验Quiz

Q1. 什么是 Prompt？Q1. What is a prompt?

Prompt就是你给AI的指令或问题。写好Prompt是高效使用AI的关键技能。A prompt is the instruction or question you give to AI. Writing good prompts is a key skill for effective AI use.

Q2. "请一步一步思考"这个技巧叫什么？Q2. What is the "think step by step" technique called?

思维链（Chain of Thought）让AI一步步推理，显著提高复杂问题的回答准确率。Chain of Thought (CoT) makes AI reason step by step, significantly improving accuracy on complex problems.

Q3. 以下哪个是好的 Prompt 写法？Q3. Which is a good prompt technique?

好的Prompt应该明确AI的角色、输出格式、目标受众等要素，让AI准确理解你的需求。Good prompts should define AI's role, output format, target audience, helping AI accurately understand your needs.

Q4. 少样本提示（Few-Shot）的特点是什么？Q4. What characterizes few-shot prompting?

少样本提示通过提供几个输入→输出的示例，让AI理解模式后完成新任务。示例帮助AI更准确地理解你的需求。Few-shot prompting provides input→output examples so AI understands the pattern, then completes new tasks. Examples help AI understand your needs better.

Q5. 使用AI时，以下哪项是不应该做的？Q5. Which should you NOT do when using AI?

把AI的回答直接当作业交是学术不诚信行为。正确做法是用AI辅助理解概念，然后用自己的话表达。Submitting AI answers as your own homework is academic dishonesty. The right approach is using AI to understand concepts, then expressing ideas in your own words.
