第 8 课Lesson 8

大语言模型揭秘Inside Large Language Models

ChatGPT 是怎么工作的？揭秘大语言模型的原理！ How does ChatGPT work? Uncover the secrets of LLMs!

8.1 从 NLP 到大语言模型8.1 From NLP to Large Language Models

大语言模型（LLM）是 NLP 的最新突破。与之前的模型相比，LLM 有几个显著特点：参数量巨大（数十亿到数万亿），能力超强，几乎什么任务都能做。 Large Language Models (LLMs) are the latest NLP breakthrough. Compared to earlier models, LLMs have key features: massive parameters (billions to trillions), incredible capability, handling almost any task.

GPT-3GPT-3

1750亿参数。2020年发布，是第一个引起广泛关注的LLM。能写文章、写代码、做翻译。175 billion parameters. Released 2020, the first LLM to gain widespread attention. Can write articles, code, and translate.

GPT-4GPT-4

参数量未公开，但能力远超GPT-3。支持图文多模态输入，通过了律师考试和医学考试。Parameter count undisclosed but far exceeds GPT-3. Supports multimodal input, passed bar and medical exams.

中文大模型Chinese LLMs

文心一言（百度）、通义千问（阿里）、讯飞星火等——中国也有很多优秀的大语言模型。ERNIE Bot (Baidu), Tongyi Qianwen (Alibaba), SparkDesk (iFlytek) — China has many excellent LLMs too.

8.2 Transformer：LLM 的核心8.2 Transformer: The Heart of LLMs

几乎所有大语言模型都基于一个叫 Transformer 的架构。它于2017年由Google提出，核心创新是注意力机制。 Almost all LLMs are based on an architecture called Transformer. Proposed by Google in 2017, its core innovation is the attention mechanism.

注意力机制是什么？想象你在读一篇长文章。当看到"她拿起了它"时，你的大脑会自动回头找"她"和"它"分别指代什么。注意力机制让AI也能做到这一点——关注句子中真正重要的部分，理解词与词之间的关系。What is attention? Imagine reading a long article. When you see "she picked it up," your brain automatically looks back to find what "she" and "it" refer to. The attention mechanism lets AI do this too — focus on truly important parts of a sentence, understanding relationships between words.

自注意力（Self-Attention）Self-Attention

让句子中的每个词都能"看到"其他所有词，计算它们之间的关联程度。比如"猫坐在垫子上，它很舒服"中，"它"会特别关注"猫"和"垫子"。Lets every word "see" all other words, calculating their relevance. In "The cat sat on the mat, it was comfortable," "it" pays special attention to "cat" and "mat."

并行处理Parallel Processing

之前的模型（如RNN）一个词一个词地处理，很慢。Transformer可以同时处理整个句子，训练速度大幅提升，让训练更大的模型成为可能。Previous models (like RNNs) processed words one by one — slow. Transformers process entire sentences simultaneously, dramatically speeding up training and enabling larger models.

8.3 LLM 是怎么"说话"的8.3 How LLMs "Talk"

LLM 本质上在做一件事：预测下一个词。当你输入"今天天气真"，它会计算出下一个词最可能是"好"、"热"还是其他词。 LLMs essentially do one thing: predict the next word. When you type "Today's weather is really," it calculates whether the next word is most likely "nice," "hot," or something else.

Step 1

接收输入Receive Input

你输入"请帮我写一首关于春天的诗"。模型把这句话转成词向量。You input "Please write me a poem about spring." The model converts this to word vectors.

Step 2

预测下一个词Predict Next Word

模型根据所有前文，计算下一个词的概率。比如"春" → 可能是"风"(40%)、"天"(30%)、"光"(15%)...Based on all prior text, the model calculates next word probabilities. Like "Spring" → could be "breeze"(40%), "day"(30%), "light"(15%)...

Step 3

逐词生成Generate Word by Word

选择一个词后，把它加入已有文本，再预测下一个词。如此反复，直到生成完整的回答。这就是为什么ChatGPT是一个字一个字"打"出来的。After choosing a word, add it to existing text, predict the next. Repeat until the full response is generated. That's why ChatGPT "types" one word at a time.

8.4 预训练与微调8.4 Pre-training & Fine-tuning

训练一个LLM分两个阶段：先学通识，再学专长。 Training an LLM has two stages: first learn general knowledge, then specialize.

预训练Pre-training

用互联网上的海量文本（书籍、网页、论文等）训练模型。就像上小学到大学——学习通用知识。Train on massive internet text (books, web pages, papers). Like elementary through university — learning general knowledge.

  • 数据量：数万亿个词Data: trillions of words
  • 成本：数百万到数千万美元Cost: millions to tens of millions USD
  • 时间：几周到几个月Time: weeks to months

微调（Fine-tuning）Fine-tuning

用特定领域的数据继续训练。就像大学毕业后读研究生——在某个方向深入。Continue training with domain-specific data. Like graduate school after university — going deep in one area.

  • RLHF：用人类反馈来优化模型RLHF: optimize with human feedback
  • 让模型更安全、更有用Makes models safer and more useful
  • 成本较低，效果显著Lower cost, significant results

8.5 LLM 的局限性8.5 Limitations of LLMs

LLM 虽然强大，但它们有一些重要的局限性，你需要了解： Despite being powerful, LLMs have important limitations you should know about:

幻觉（Hallucination）Hallucination

LLM 会"一本正经地胡说八道"——用非常自信的语气说出完全错误的信息。比如编造不存在的论文和引用。这是目前最大的问题。LLMs can "confidently make things up" — stating completely false information with a confident tone. Like fabricating non-existent papers. This is currently the biggest problem.

知识截止Knowledge Cutoff

LLM 只知道训练数据中的信息，不了解训练后发生的事情。问它"昨天的新闻"，它无法回答。LLMs only know information from their training data. They can't answer about events after their training cutoff date.

不会真正"思考"Can't Truly "Think"

LLM 只是在做统计预测——预测最可能的下一个词。它不理解含义，没有意识，不会真正推理。LLMs just do statistical prediction — predicting the most likely next word. They don't understand meaning, lack consciousness, and can't truly reason.

偏见和有害内容Bias & Harmful Content

因为训练数据来自互联网，LLM 可能包含偏见、歧视性内容。虽然经过安全过滤，但仍可能被诱导产生有害输出。Since training data comes from the internet, LLMs may contain biases and discriminatory content. Despite safety filters, they can still be tricked into harmful outputs.

重要提醒：永远不要100%信任LLM的回答！对于重要的事实、数据和建议，一定要自己核实。把LLM当作一个很有用但可能犯错的助手。Important: Never trust LLM answers 100%! For important facts, data, and advice, always verify yourself. Treat LLMs as a very useful but error-prone assistant.

8.6 小测验8.6 Quiz

让我们来检验一下你在本课中学到的知识！Let's test what you've learned in this lesson!

Q1. 大语言模型本质上在做什么？Q1. What do LLMs fundamentally do?

LLM的核心原理是预测下一个词——根据已有文本，计算每个可能的下一个词的概率，选择最合适的。LLM's core principle is next-word prediction — calculating probability of each possible next word based on existing text, choosing the best.

Q2. Transformer 的核心创新是什么？Q2. What is Transformer's core innovation?

注意力机制让模型能关注句子中最相关的部分，理解词与词之间的远距离依赖关系，是Transformer的核心创新。Attention lets the model focus on most relevant parts, understanding long-range word dependencies — Transformer's core innovation.

Q3. "幻觉"（Hallucination）是什么意思？Q3. What does "hallucination" mean in LLM context?

LLM的"幻觉"是指它用非常自信的语气说出完全错误或编造的信息，比如虚构不存在的引用和事实。LLM "hallucination" means confidently outputting completely false or fabricated information, like made-up citations and facts.
