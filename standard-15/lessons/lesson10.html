AIGC 创意工坊AIGC Creative Workshop

AI 生成内容——用人工智能释放你的创造力！ AI Generated Content — unleash your creativity with artificial intelligence!

10.1 什么是 AIGC？10.1 What is AIGC?

AIGC（AI Generated Content，AI 生成内容）是指用人工智能自动生成文字、图片、音乐、视频等创意内容。从前只有人类能创作的东西，现在AI也能做了——而且速度快得惊人。 AIGC (AI Generated Content) refers to using AI to automatically generate creative content like text, images, music, and video. Things that only humans could create before, AI can now do — at astonishing speed.

AI 文本生成AI Text Generation

ChatGPT、文心一言等可以写文章、写诗、写代码、做翻译。从100字的微博到10000字的论文，几秒钟就能完成。ChatGPT, ERNIE Bot can write articles, poems, code, translations. From 100-word posts to 10,000-word papers, done in seconds.

AI 图像生成AI Image Generation

Midjourney、DALL-E、Stable Diffusion——输入文字描述，AI就能画出相应的图片。从写实照片到动漫风格，无所不能。Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion — type a description and AI draws the image. From photorealistic to anime style, anything is possible.

AI 音乐生成AI Music Generation

Suno、Udio 等工具可以根据你的描述生成完整的歌曲，包括旋律、编曲，甚至歌词和人声。Tools like Suno and Udio can generate complete songs from your description, including melody, arrangement, lyrics, and vocals.

AI 视频生成AI Video Generation

Sora、Runway 等可以根据文字描述生成逼真的视频片段。AI 电影和广告正在成为现实。Sora, Runway can generate realistic video clips from text descriptions. AI movies and ads are becoming reality.

10.2 AI 绘画原理：扩散模型10.2 AI Art: Diffusion Models

目前最流行的AI绘画技术基于扩散模型。它的原理很有趣： The most popular AI art technology is based on diffusion models. The principle is fascinating:

Step 1

加噪声（训练时）Add Noise (Training)

拿一张清晰图片，逐步加上随机噪声，直到变成完全的"雪花屏"。AI学习这个过程中每一步的变化。Take a clear image, gradually add random noise until it becomes pure "static." AI learns the transformation at each step.

Step 2

去噪声（生成时）Remove Noise (Generation)

反过来！从一张随机噪声图开始，AI一步步去除噪声，逐渐"画"出一张清晰的图片。Reverse it! Starting from random noise, AI removes noise step by step, gradually "painting" a clear image.

Step 3

文字引导Text Guidance

你的文字描述（prompt）引导去噪过程的方向。输入"日落海边"，AI在去噪时就会朝着海边日落的方向生成图片。Your text description (prompt) guides the denoising direction. Input "sunset by the sea" and AI generates toward that scene during denoising.

10.3 AI 绘画 Prompt 技巧10.3 AI Art Prompt Tips

想让AI画出好看的图，prompt写法很关键： To get AI to create beautiful images, prompt writing is crucial:

描述主体Describe Subject

"一只橘猫坐在窗台上"比"猫"具体得多。描述动作、表情、颜色、数量等细节。"An orange cat sitting on a windowsill" is much more specific than "cat." Describe actions, expressions, colors, and details.

指定风格Specify Style

加上艺术风格："水彩画风"、"赛博朋克"、"宫崎骏风格"、"写实摄影"，AI会按照对应风格生成。Add art style: "watercolor," "cyberpunk," "Studio Ghibli style," "photorealistic" — AI generates in that style.

设定氛围Set the Mood

"温暖的日落光线"、"神秘的蓝色调"、"欢快明亮的色彩"——氛围词能极大影响画面效果。"Warm sunset lighting," "mysterious blue tones," "cheerful bright colors" — mood words greatly influence the result.

技术参数Technical Parameters

"8K高清"、"景深效果"、"电影感构图"、"黄金比例"——这些技术词能提升画面质量和专业感。"8K HD," "depth of field," "cinematic composition," "golden ratio" — technical terms boost quality and professionalism.

10.4 AIGC 的争议10.4 AIGC Controversies

AIGC 带来了巨大的创造力革命，但也引发了很多争议： AIGC brings a huge creative revolution but also sparks many controversies:

版权问题Copyright Issues

AI 用已有的艺术作品训练，生成的图片算谁的？原画师的权益如何保护？这是目前最大的法律争议。AI trains on existing art — who owns the generated images? How to protect original artists' rights? This is the biggest legal debate.

深度伪造Deepfakes

AI 可以生成以假乱真的人脸照片和视频，可能被用于诈骗、传播虚假信息。如何防范是个重大挑战。AI can generate hyper-realistic face photos and videos, potentially used for scams and misinformation. Prevention is a major challenge.

职业影响Career Impact

插画师、设计师、写手等创意工作者的岗位是否会被AI取代？还是AI只是一种新工具？Will AI replace illustrators, designers, and writers? Or is AI just a new tool? The debate continues.

学术诚信Academic Integrity

用AI写作文、做作业算不算作弊？学校该如何对待AIGC？不同学校的态度也不一样。Is using AI for essays and homework cheating? How should schools handle AIGC? Different schools have different views.

10.5 AIGC 的正确使用方式10.5 Using AIGC the Right Way

核心原则：把AI当作你的创意助手，而不是替代品。AI帮你加速创意过程，但核心创意和判断力还是来自你自己。Core principle: Treat AI as your creative assistant, not a replacement. AI accelerates the creative process, but core creativity and judgment still come from you.

  • 学习辅助：用AI生成学习笔记的框架，然后自己填充内容。用AI练习外语对话。Study aid: Use AI to generate note frameworks, then fill in content yourself. Practice foreign language conversation.

  • 创意启发：让AI生成创意点子的列表，从中选择和改进。用AI做头脑风暴的伙伴。Creative inspiration: Let AI generate idea lists to choose from and improve. Use AI as a brainstorming partner.

  • 原型设计：用AI快速生成设计概念图、故事大纲、音乐demo，然后在此基础上精细创作。Prototyping: Use AI to quickly generate concept art, story outlines, music demos, then refine from there.

小测验Quiz

Q1. AIGC 的全称是什么？Q1. What does AIGC stand for?

AIGC = AI Generated Content，即AI生成内容，包括文字、图片、音乐、视频等各种创意内容。AIGC = AI Generated Content, including text, images, music, video, and all kinds of creative content.

Q2. 扩散模型生成图片的核心原理是什么？Q2. What is the core principle of diffusion models?

扩散模型从随机噪声开始，通过学习到的去噪过程，一步步生成清晰的图片。文字描述引导去噪方向。Diffusion models start from random noise and generate clear images step by step through learned denoising. Text descriptions guide the denoising direction.

Q3. 以下哪个是好的 AI 绘画提示词？Q3. Which is a good AI art prompt?

好的AI绘画prompt应该包含主体描述、艺术风格和氛围。越具体越能得到你想要的效果。Good AI art prompts should include subject description, art style, and atmosphere. The more specific, the closer to your desired result.

Q4. AIGC引发的最大版权争议是什么？Q4. What is the biggest copyright controversy of AIGC?

AI用大量已有的艺术作品训练，但生成的图片版权归谁？原画师的权益如何保护？这是目前最大的法律和伦理争议。AI trains on massive existing artwork, but who owns the generated images? How to protect original artists? This is the biggest legal and ethical debate.

Q5. 使用 AIGC 的正确方式是什么？Q5. What is the right way to use AIGC?

AI是强大的创意助手，但核心创意和判断力应该来自你自己。用AI加速创意过程，而不是替代你的思考。AI is a powerful creative assistant, but core creativity and judgment should come from you. Use AI to accelerate creative processes, not replace your thinking.
