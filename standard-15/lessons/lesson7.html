第 7 课Lesson 7

自然语言处理Natural Language Processing

教机器读懂人类的语言——NLP 是如何工作的？ Teaching machines to understand human language — how does NLP work?

7.1 为什么语言这么难？7.1 Why is Language So Hard?

你可能觉得理解语言很简单——毕竟你每天都在用。但对计算机来说，语言是世界上最复杂的东西之一。为什么？ You might think understanding language is easy — after all, you use it every day. But for computers, language is one of the most complex things in the world. Why?

歧义性Ambiguity

"苹果"是水果还是手机品牌？"他在银行工作"中的"银行"是河岸还是金融机构？同一个词在不同语境中含义完全不同。"Apple" — a fruit or a phone brand? "He sat on the bank" — a river bank or a financial institution? The same word means completely different things in different contexts.

隐含意义Implied Meaning

"今天天气真好啊"——说这话的人可能真的在夸天气，也可能是在讽刺（比如暴雨天说的）。人类靠语境理解，但机器很难判断。"Nice weather today" — could be genuine or sarcastic (said during a storm). Humans use context to understand, but machines struggle with this.

语法灵活性Grammar Flexibility

"我吃了饭"和"饭被我吃了"意思一样，但结构完全不同。中文还没有时态变化，全靠语境判断时间。"I ate food" and "food was eaten by me" mean the same but have completely different structures. Chinese also lacks tense markers, relying entirely on context for time.

网络用语Internet Slang

"yyds"、"绝绝子"、"duck不必"——网络用语变化极快，AI需要不断学习新词新用法。这对机器来说是个巨大挑战。"GOAT," "slay," "no cap" — internet slang evolves rapidly. AI needs to constantly learn new words and usages, a huge challenge for machines.

7.2 分词与词向量7.2 Tokenization & Word Vectors

计算机不认识文字，只认识数字。所以NLP的第一步是把文字转换成数字。 Computers don't understand text, only numbers. So the first step in NLP is converting text to numbers.

分词（Tokenization）Tokenization

把句子拆成一个个词语。英语很简单（用空格分开），但中文"我喜欢机器学习"需要AI来判断应该分成"我/喜欢/机器/学习"还是"我/喜欢/机器学习"。Breaking sentences into individual words. English is simple (split by spaces), but Chinese "我喜欢机器学习" requires AI to decide the right split — is it "machine learning" or "machine" + "learning"?

词向量（Word Embedding）Word Embedding

把每个词变成一组数字（向量）。神奇的是，意思相近的词在数字空间中"距离"也近。"国王-男人+女人≈女王"这个等式在词向量中是成立的！Turn each word into a set of numbers (vector). Amazingly, similar meanings are "close" in number space. The equation "king - man + woman ≈ queen" actually works in word vectors!

想象一下：词向量就像给每个词在一个高维空间中分配了一个"座位"。意思相近的词坐在一起，相反的词隔得远。这让机器能"感受"到词语之间的关系。Imagine: Word vectors are like assigning each word a "seat" in a high-dimensional space. Similar words sit together; opposite words are far apart. This lets machines "feel" relationships between words.

7.3 情感分析7.3 Sentiment Analysis

情感分析是NLP最常见的应用之一——让AI判断一段文字表达的是正面还是负面情感。电商平台用它来分析用户评价，社交媒体用它来检测网络暴力。 Sentiment analysis is one of NLP's most common applications — letting AI judge whether text expresses positive or negative feelings. E-commerce uses it for reviews; social media uses it to detect cyberbullying.

正面情感Positive Sentiment

  • "这部电影太精彩了，强烈推荐！""This movie is amazing, highly recommend!"
  • "服务态度超好，下次还来！""Great service, will come back!"
  • "食物很好吃，分量也足""Food is delicious and portions are generous"

负面情感Negative Sentiment

  • "等了两小时才上菜，太差了""Waited 2 hours for food, terrible"
  • "质量很差，完全不值这个价""Poor quality, not worth the price at all"
  • "客服态度极其恶劣""Customer service attitude was awful"

7.4 机器翻译的进化7.4 Evolution of Machine Translation

机器翻译是NLP最经典的应用之一。从最早的逐词翻译到今天的AI翻译，经历了巨大的进化。 Machine translation is one of NLP's most classic applications. From early word-by-word translation to today's AI translation, it has undergone huge evolution.

1950s

规则翻译Rule-Based

人工编写语法规则和词典，逐词翻译。质量很差，经常闹笑话。Manually written grammar rules and dictionaries, word-by-word translation. Very poor quality, often laughable.

2000s

统计翻译Statistical MT

从大量已翻译的文本中学习翻译规律。质量提升明显，但仍然经常不通顺。Learn translation patterns from large amounts of translated text. Notable quality improvement, but still often unnatural.

2017+

神经网络翻译Neural MT

基于Transformer架构的神经网络翻译，质量接近人类水平。Google翻译、DeepL都使用这种技术。Transformer-based neural network translation, approaching human-level quality. Google Translate and DeepL both use this technology.

7.5 NLP 的更多应用7.5 More NLP Applications

NLP 的应用远不止翻译和情感分析。看看你可能每天都在使用的 NLP 技术： NLP applications go far beyond translation and sentiment analysis. Check out NLP tech you probably use daily:

搜索引擎Search Engines

百度、Google 理解你搜索的意图，即使你打错字或表达不清楚，也能给出相关结果。Baidu, Google understand your search intent, giving relevant results even with typos or unclear phrasing.

智能客服Smart Customer Service

网站和App里的AI客服机器人，能理解你的问题并自动回答，处理退货、查物流等常见问题。AI chatbots in websites and apps understand your questions and auto-respond, handling returns, tracking, and common issues.

语音转文字Speech-to-Text

讯飞输入法、微信语音转文字——把你说的话准确地转换成文字，准确率已超过97%。Speech-to-text tools convert your speech to text with over 97% accuracy — making notes, subtitles, and accessibility possible.

Q1. 对计算机来说，理解语言最大的困难之一是什么？Q1. What is one of the biggest challenges for computers in understanding language?

语言的歧义性是NLP最大的挑战之一。同一个词（如"苹果"）在不同语境中可能有完全不同的含义。Language ambiguity is one of NLP's biggest challenges. The same word (like "apple") can have completely different meanings in different contexts.

Q2. 词向量的神奇之处是什么？Q2. What's magical about word vectors?

词向量把语义关系转化为空间关系——同义词距离近，反义词距离远。"国王-男人+女人≈女王"就是一个经典例子。Word vectors convert semantic relationships to spatial ones — synonyms are close, antonyms are far. "King - man + woman ≈ queen" is a classic example.

Q3. 情感分析的主要功能是什么？Q3. What is the main function of sentiment analysis?

情感分析让AI判断文本中表达的情绪是正面、负面还是中性，广泛用于评价分析和社交媒体监控。Sentiment analysis lets AI judge whether text emotion is positive, negative, or neutral, widely used in review analysis and social media monitoring.
