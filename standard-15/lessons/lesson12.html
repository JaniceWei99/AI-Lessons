第 12 课Lesson 12

强化学习与游戏AIReinforcement Learning & Game AI

AI 如何通过"玩游戏"学会超越人类？ How does AI learn to surpass humans by "playing games"?

12.1 什么是强化学习？12.1 What is Reinforcement Learning?

强化学习（RL）是一种特殊的机器学习方法。与监督学习不同，RL不需要标注好的数据——它通过试错来学习。就像训练小狗一样：做对了给奖励（骨头），做错了给惩罚（不给骨头）。 Reinforcement Learning (RL) is a special machine learning method. Unlike supervised learning, RL doesn't need labeled data — it learns by trial and error. Like training a puppy: good behavior gets rewards (treats), bad behavior gets penalties (no treats).

智能体（Agent）Agent

做决策的AI。在游戏中就是AI玩家，在机器人中就是机器人的"大脑"。The AI making decisions. In games, it's the AI player; in robots, it's the robot's "brain."

环境（Environment）Environment

智能体所在的世界。可以是游戏地图、棋盘、真实世界。环境会根据智能体的行动发生变化。The world the agent lives in. Could be a game map, chess board, or real world. It changes based on agent actions.

奖励（Reward）Reward

环境给智能体的反馈信号。得分+1是奖励，失去生命是惩罚。智能体的目标是最大化总奖励。Feedback signal from environment. Score +1 is reward, losing a life is penalty. The agent's goal: maximize total reward.

12.2 探索与利用12.2 Exploration vs. Exploitation

强化学习面临一个核心困境：探索还是利用？ RL faces a core dilemma: explore or exploit?

探索（Exploration）Exploration

尝试新的、没试过的行动。可能发现更好的策略，也可能浪费时间。就像去一家没去过的餐厅——可能发现新美食，也可能踩雷。Try new, untested actions. Might discover better strategies, or waste time. Like trying a new restaurant — might find great food, might be disappointing.

利用（Exploitation）Exploitation

重复已知的最佳行动。安全但可能错过更好的选择。就像永远去同一家最爱的餐厅——稳定但可能错过更好吃的店。Repeat the best known action. Safe but might miss better options. Like always going to your favorite restaurant — reliable but might miss better ones.

平衡是关键：好的RL算法会在开始时多探索（了解环境），随着学习的深入逐渐转向利用（使用最佳策略）。就像你在一个新城市——刚到时到处探索，熟悉后就知道去哪里最好了。Balance is key: Good RL algorithms explore more at the start (learning the environment) and gradually shift to exploitation (using the best strategy). Like arriving in a new city — explore at first, then you know the best spots.

12.3 AI 击败人类的里程碑12.3 AI Beating Humans: Milestones

1997

深蓝 vs 卡斯帕罗夫Deep Blue vs. Kasparov

IBM的"深蓝"击败国际象棋世界冠军。但深蓝主要靠暴力搜索（每秒分析2亿个棋局），不是真正的"学习"。IBM's Deep Blue beat the world chess champion. But Deep Blue relied on brute-force search (analyzing 200 million positions/second), not true "learning."

2016

AlphaGo vs 李世石AlphaGo vs. Lee Sedol

DeepMind的AlphaGo以4:1击败围棋世界冠军。围棋的复杂度远超象棋（可能的棋局数超过宇宙中原子的数量），暴力搜索行不通。AlphaGo结合了深度学习和强化学习，真正"学会"了下棋。DeepMind's AlphaGo beat Go world champion 4-1. Go's complexity far exceeds chess (more possible games than atoms in the universe), making brute force impossible. AlphaGo combined deep learning and RL, truly "learning" to play.

2019

AlphaStar 征服星际争霸AlphaStar Conquers StarCraft

AI在星际争霸II中达到大师级水平。这是一个需要即时决策、长期规划、不完全信息的复杂游戏，比棋类难得多。AI reached Grandmaster level in StarCraft II. This game requires real-time decisions, long-term planning, and incomplete information — far harder than board games.

2023+

AI 征服更多游戏AI Conquers More Games

AI已经在几乎所有经典游戏中超越人类：Dota 2、扑克、麻将等。下一个挑战是更复杂的开放世界游戏。AI has surpassed humans in nearly all classic games: Dota 2, poker, mahjong. Next challenge: more complex open-world games.

12.4 RL 不只是游戏12.4 RL Beyond Games

虽然游戏是RL最出名的应用，但它的用途远不止于此： While games are RL's most famous application, its uses go far beyond:

机器人控制Robot Control

教机器人走路、抓取物体、完成复杂动作。RL让机器人通过反复试验学会这些技能，而不需要人工编程每一个动作。Teach robots to walk, grasp objects, and perform complex actions. RL lets robots learn skills through trial and error, without manually programming each movement.

推荐系统Recommendation Systems

抖音、B站的推荐算法就用了RL——通过观察你的点赞、评论、观看时长来学习你的喜好，不断优化推荐内容。TikTok and YouTube recommendation algorithms use RL — learning your preferences from likes, comments, and watch time to continuously optimize recommendations.

自动驾驶Self-Driving

RL帮助自动驾驶系统做决策：何时变道、何时刹车、如何处理复杂路口。在模拟环境中训练数百万次后再上路。RL helps self-driving systems decide: when to change lanes, brake, handle complex intersections. Trained millions of times in simulation before hitting the road.

RLHF 优化LLMRLHF for LLMs

ChatGPT就用了RLHF（基于人类反馈的强化学习）。人类评价AI的回答，RL根据这些反馈不断改进模型。ChatGPT uses RLHF (Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback). Humans rate AI answers, and RL continuously improves the model based on this feedback.

12.5 设计你自己的RL环境12.5 Design Your Own RL Environment

理解RL最好的方式是思考如何设计一个RL系统。试着回答这些问题： The best way to understand RL is thinking about how to design an RL system. Try answering these questions:

思考练习：如果你要设计一个AI来学会打乒乓球：Thought exercise: If you were designing an AI to learn ping pong:

  • 智能体是什么？控制球拍的AI程序Agent? AI program controlling the paddle

  • 环境是什么？乒乓球桌、球、对手Environment? Ping pong table, ball, opponent

  • 状态包括什么？球的位置和速度、球拍位置、对手位置State includes? Ball position and speed, paddle position, opponent position

  • 动作有哪些？上移、下移、击球力度和角度Actions? Move up, move down, hit strength and angle

  • 奖励是什么？得分+1，失分-1Rewards? Score point +1, lose point -1

小测验Quiz

Q1. 强化学习的核心学习方式是什么？Q1. What is RL's core learning method?

强化学习通过不断尝试动作、接收环境反馈（奖励/惩罚），逐步学会最优策略。不需要预先标注的数据。RL learns by repeatedly trying actions and receiving environmental feedback (rewards/penalties), gradually learning the optimal strategy without pre-labeled data.

Q2. "探索vs利用"困境中，"利用"指的是什么？Q2. In the "explore vs exploit" dilemma, what does "exploit" mean?

"利用"是指重复已知最优的行动策略。虽然安全，但可能错过更好的选择。好的RL会在探索与利用之间找到平衡。"Exploit" means repeating the best known strategy. Safe but might miss better options. Good RL finds a balance between exploration and exploitation.

Q3. AlphaGo 为什么比深蓝更厉害？Q3. Why was AlphaGo more impressive than Deep Blue?

深蓝靠暴力搜索赢棋，而AlphaGo结合深度学习和强化学习真正"理解"了围棋的策略。围棋的复杂度远超象棋，暴力搜索行不通。Deep Blue won through brute force; AlphaGo combined deep learning and RL to truly "understand" Go strategy. Go's complexity far exceeds chess, making brute force impossible.

Q4. 以下哪个不是强化学习的三个核心要素？Q4. Which is NOT one of RL's three core elements?

RL的三个核心要素是智能体、环境和奖励。RL不需要预先准备的数据集——它通过与环境交互自己产生"数据"。RL's three core elements are Agent, Environment, and Reward. RL doesn't need prepared datasets — it generates its own "data" through environmental interaction.

Q5. RLHF 在 ChatGPT 中的作用是什么？Q5. What role does RLHF play in ChatGPT?

RLHF让人类评价ChatGPT的回答，然后用强化学习根据这些反馈优化模型，使回答更安全、更有用、更符合人类期望。RLHF has humans evaluate ChatGPT responses, then uses RL to optimize the model based on feedback, making answers safer, more useful, and aligned with human expectations.
