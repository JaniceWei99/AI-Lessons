第 1 课 Lesson 1

AI 就在你身边 AI Is All Around You

推荐算法、人脸识别、语音助手、自动驾驶——AI 早已融入你的日常生活 Recommendation algorithms, face recognition, voice assistants, self-driving — AI is already part of your daily life

1.1 早上好，AI！ 1.1 Good Morning, AI!

想象一下你的一天：早上醒来，用人脸识别解锁手机；打开短视频App，推荐算法为你推送感兴趣的内容；对着手机说"今天天气怎么样"，语音助手立刻回答你。这一切的背后，都是人工智能在默默工作。 Imagine your day: You wake up and unlock your phone with face recognition; open a video app and the recommendation algorithm feeds you content you'll love; ask your phone "What's the weather today?" and the voice assistant answers instantly. Behind all of this, AI is working silently.

你可能觉得 AI 是科幻电影里才有的东西，但事实是，AI 已经像水和电一样融入了我们的生活。让我们一起来看看，你每天都在使用哪些 AI 技术吧！ You might think AI is something from sci-fi movies, but the truth is, AI has become as integrated into our lives as water and electricity. Let's explore what AI technologies you use every single day!

1.2 推荐算法：比你更懂你 1.2 Recommendation Algorithms: They Know You Better Than You

你有没有想过，为什么抖音、B站、小红书总能推荐你喜欢的内容？这就是推荐算法的魔力。 Have you ever wondered why TikTok, YouTube, or Instagram always seem to recommend content you love? That's the magic of recommendation algorithms.

记录你的行为 Tracking Your Behavior

AI 会记住你看了哪些视频、点了哪些赞、停留了多久，从而建立一个"你的兴趣模型"。 AI remembers which videos you watch, what you like, and how long you stay, building a "model of your interests."

找到"相似的人" Finding "Similar People"

AI 还会找到跟你兴趣相似的其他用户，把他们喜欢的内容推荐给你——这叫协同过滤。 AI also finds other users with similar interests and recommends what they like to you — this is called collaborative filtering.

想一想：推荐算法让我们更容易找到好内容，但也可能让我们陷入"信息茧房"——只看到自己喜欢的东西，看不到不同的观点。你觉得这是好事还是坏事？ Think about it: Recommendation algorithms help us find great content, but they can also trap us in a "filter bubble" — only seeing what we already like, missing different perspectives. Do you think this is a good thing or a bad thing?

1.3 人脸识别与语音助手 1.3 Face Recognition & Voice Assistants

每次你用脸解锁手机，背后发生了什么？ Every time you unlock your phone with your face, what happens behind the scenes?

人脸识别三步曲 Face Recognition in 3 Steps

1. 检测：找到图像中的人脸位置
2. 提取：分析眼睛、鼻子、嘴巴等特征点
3. 匹配：与已存储的人脸数据进行对比 1. Detect: Find the face location in the image
2. Extract: Analyze feature points like eyes, nose, mouth
3. Match: Compare with stored facial data

语音助手如何工作 How Voice Assistants Work

1. 语音识别：把你说的话转成文字
2. 自然语言理解：理解你的意图
3. 生成回答：用语音合成技术回复你 1. Speech Recognition: Convert your speech to text
2. NLU: Understand your intent
3. Generate Response: Reply using speech synthesis

从 Siri 到小爱同学，从 Alexa 到天猫精灵，语音助手已经能理解复杂的指令、控制智能家居、甚至跟你聊天。随着大语言模型的发展，它们正变得越来越"聪明"。 From Siri to Alexa, from Google Assistant to smart speakers, voice assistants can now understand complex commands, control smart homes, and even chat with you. With the development of large language models, they're getting "smarter" every day.

1.4 自动驾驶：AI 的终极挑战 1.4 Self-Driving: AI's Ultimate Challenge

自动驾驶被认为是 AI 最具挑战性的应用之一。一辆自动驾驶汽车需要同时完成多项 AI 任务： Self-driving is considered one of AI's most challenging applications. An autonomous vehicle must perform multiple AI tasks simultaneously:

感知环境 Perceive

用摄像头和雷达识别路上的车辆、行人、交通标志 Use cameras and radar to detect vehicles, pedestrians, and traffic signs

做出决策 Decide

判断是加速、减速还是转弯，预测其他车辆的行为 Decide to accelerate, brake, or turn, and predict other vehicles' behavior

执行操作 Execute

控制方向盘、油门和刹车，精确到毫秒级响应 Control steering, throttle, and brakes with millisecond-level response

自动驾驶分为 L1 到 L5 共五个等级。目前大多数量产车处于 L2（辅助驾驶）水平，特斯拉、华为、小鹏等公司正在向 L3-L4 迈进。完全无人驾驶（L5）仍然是未来的目标。 Self-driving is classified into 5 levels (L1-L5). Most production cars are at L2 (assisted driving). Companies like Tesla, Waymo, and others are pushing toward L3-L4. Fully autonomous driving (L5) remains a future goal.

1.5 小测验 1.5 Quiz

Q1. 短视频平台为你推荐感兴趣的内容，主要依靠什么技术？ Q1. What technology do video platforms mainly use to recommend content you like?

推荐算法通过分析你的浏览行为和兴趣偏好，为你精准推送感兴趣的内容。 Recommendation algorithms analyze your browsing behavior and interest preferences to precisely push content you'll enjoy.

Q2. 人脸识别的正确步骤顺序是什么？ Q2. What is the correct order of face recognition steps?

人脸识别的三个步骤是：先检测（找到人脸位置），再提取（分析特征点），最后匹配（与已有数据对比）。 The three steps are: Detect (find face location), Extract (analyze feature points), then Match (compare with stored data).

Q3. 目前大多数量产自动驾驶汽车处于哪个等级？ Q3. What level are most production self-driving cars at currently?

目前大多数量产车处于 L2 辅助驾驶水平，驾驶员仍需时刻关注路况并随时准备接管。 Most production cars are at L2 assisted driving, where drivers must still pay attention and be ready to take over at any time.
