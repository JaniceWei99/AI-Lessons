第 13 课Lesson 13

AI 伦理与偏见AI Ethics & Bias

AI 也会犯错和歧视？理解人工智能的伦理挑战 Can AI be wrong and discriminatory? Understanding AI's ethical challenges

13.1 AI 中的偏见从何而来？13.1 Where Does AI Bias Come From?

AI 本身没有偏见——但它的训练数据可能有。偏见的数据进去，偏见的结果出来（Garbage In, Garbage Out）。如果训练数据中存在性别、种族、年龄等偏见，AI就会"学到"这些偏见。 AI itself has no bias — but its training data might. Biased data in, biased results out (Garbage In, Garbage Out). If training data contains gender, racial, or age biases, AI will "learn" these biases.

数据偏见Data Bias

如果人脸识别系统主要用白人面孔训练，它识别深色皮肤的准确率就会低很多。真实案例：某些人脸识别系统对深色皮肤女性的错误率高达34%，而对浅色皮肤男性只有0.8%。If a face recognition system trains mostly on white faces, accuracy for darker skin tones drops significantly. Real case: some systems had 34% error for dark-skinned women vs 0.8% for light-skinned men.

社会偏见Social Bias

AI招聘系统曾经学会了"男性比女性更适合当程序员"的偏见——因为历史数据中程序员确实以男性为主。但这不代表女性不适合编程。AI hiring systems once learned the bias "men are better programmers than women" — because historical data showed more male programmers. But that doesn't mean women can't code.

标签偏见Label Bias

给数据打标签的人也有偏见。如果标注者认为"CEO都是男性"，他们标注的数据就会带有这种偏见，AI就会学到这个错误的规律。People who label data also have biases. If annotators assume "CEOs are male," their labeled data carries this bias, and AI learns this incorrect pattern.

选择偏见Selection Bias

训练数据可能只代表部分人群。用英文互联网数据训练的AI对英语文化的理解好，但可能完全不了解其他文化。Training data may only represent some groups. AI trained on English internet data understands English culture well but may completely miss other cultures.

13.2 真实的AI偏见事件13.2 Real AI Bias Incidents

2015

Google Photos 的种族错误Google Photos Racial Error

Google Photos 的图像识别系统把黑人用户的照片标记为"大猩猩"。这是因为训练数据中深色皮肤的面部数据严重不足。Google 紧急修复了这个问题。Google Photos' image recognition labeled photos of Black users as "gorillas." This was because training data severely lacked dark-skinned facial data. Google urgently fixed this issue.

2018

亚马逊AI招聘歧视女性Amazon AI Hiring Discriminated Women

亚马逊开发的AI招聘系统自动降低女性应聘者的评分。因为历史数据中科技公司员工以男性为主，AI错误地学会了"男性 = 更好的候选人"。亚马逊最终放弃了这个系统。Amazon's AI hiring system automatically downgraded female applicants. Since historical tech employees were mostly male, AI incorrectly learned "male = better candidate." Amazon eventually abandoned the system.

2020

医疗AI偏见Medical AI Bias

用于预测患者健康风险的AI系统对黑人患者的关注度低于白人患者——因为训练数据基于"医疗花费"，而美国黑人的平均医疗花费因系统性原因低于白人。An AI predicting patient health risks gave less attention to Black patients — because training data was based on "healthcare spending," which was systematically lower for Black Americans.

13.3 AI 伦理的核心原则13.3 Core Principles of AI Ethics

公平性Fairness

AI 应该对所有人公平，不因种族、性别、年龄等因素区别对待。这需要多样化的训练数据和公平性测试。AI should be fair to everyone, not discriminating based on race, gender, age, etc. This requires diverse training data and fairness testing.

透明性Transparency

AI的决策过程应该可以解释。当AI拒绝你的贷款申请时，你有权知道原因，而不是面对一个"黑箱"。AI decision-making should be explainable. When AI denies your loan application, you deserve to know why, not face a "black box."

责任性Accountability

AI犯错时谁负责？开发者、使用者、还是AI本身？需要明确的责任框架。目前的共识是：AI不能为自己的决定负责，人类必须承担最终责任。Who's responsible when AI errs? Developer, user, or AI? Clear accountability frameworks are needed. Current consensus: AI can't be responsible for its decisions — humans must bear ultimate responsibility.

安全性Safety

AI系统不应该对人类造成伤害。在医疗、交通等高风险领域，AI的安全性尤其重要。AI systems should not harm humans. In high-risk areas like healthcare and transportation, AI safety is especially critical.

13.4 可解释AI（XAI）13.4 Explainable AI (XAI)

深度学习模型像一个"黑箱"——我们知道它给出了答案，但不知道它是怎么想的。可解释AI（XAI）试图打开这个黑箱： Deep learning models are like a "black box" — we know they give answers but don't know how they think. Explainable AI (XAI) tries to open this black box:

不可解释的AIUnexplainable AI

"你的贷款被拒绝了。""Your loan is denied."

（为什么？不知道。AI说了算。）(Why? Unknown. AI decided.)

可解释的AIExplainable AI

"你的贷款被拒绝了，主要原因是：1)信用记录中有逾期；2)收入与贷款金额比例偏低。""Your loan is denied. Main reasons: 1) Late payments in credit history; 2) Income-to-loan ratio is low."

13.5 你能做什么？13.5 What Can You Do?

作为AI时代的青少年，你可以：As a teen in the AI era, you can:

  • 保持批判思维：不要盲目相信AI的输出，特别是涉及人的判断时Think critically: Don't blindly trust AI outputs, especially when they involve judging people

  • 了解偏见：知道AI可能存在偏见，理解偏见的来源和影响Understand bias: Know AI can be biased, understand where bias comes from and its impact

  • 多元参与：未来如果你参与AI开发，确保团队多样化、数据多样化Diverse participation: If you build AI in the future, ensure diverse teams and diverse data

  • 发声：当发现AI系统存在偏见或不公平时，勇于指出和报告Speak up: When you find biased or unfair AI systems, be brave enough to point it out and report it

小测验Quiz

Q1. AI偏见最主要的来源是什么？Q1. What is the main source of AI bias?

AI偏见主要来自训练数据——偏见的数据进去，偏见的结果出来（GIGO原则）。AI本身没有价值观或偏见。AI bias mainly comes from training data — biased data in, biased results out (GIGO principle). AI itself has no values or biases.

Q2. 亚马逊AI招聘系统的问题是什么？Q2. What was the problem with Amazon's AI hiring system?

因为历史数据中科技公司员工以男性为主，AI学到了"男性更优秀"的错误偏见，自动给女性更低的评分。Since historical tech employees were mostly male, AI learned the false bias "men are better," automatically giving women lower scores.

Q3. "可解释AI"(XAI)的目标是什么？Q3. What is the goal of "Explainable AI" (XAI)?

XAI致力于打开AI的"黑箱"，让人们理解AI为什么做出某个决策，特别是在高风险领域（医疗、法律、金融）中。XAI aims to open AI's "black box," helping people understand why AI made a decision, especially in high-risk areas (healthcare, law, finance).

Q4. AI犯错时谁应该承担最终责任？Q4. Who should bear ultimate responsibility when AI makes mistakes?

当前共识是AI不能为自己的决定负责，人类必须承担最终责任——包括开发AI的公司和使用AI的个人或组织。Current consensus: AI can't be responsible for its decisions. Humans must bear ultimate responsibility — including companies developing AI and individuals/organizations using it.

Q5. 面对AI偏见，青少年能做什么？Q5. What can teens do about AI bias?

面对AI偏见，最重要的是保持批判思维，不盲目相信AI的输出，发现问题时勇于指出和报告。Facing AI bias, the most important thing is to think critically, don't blindly trust AI outputs, and be brave enough to report issues when found.
